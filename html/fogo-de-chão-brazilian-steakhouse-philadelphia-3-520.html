    While I've been at Fogo several times, each time has been a good experience. Our server, Bill was prompt and courteous and made sure that everyone in our party had everything they needed. After the first 30 minutes with no lamb, we asked our server if he could get one of them over there. No one came, so we asked politely a second time an hour later - still, no one came. Our third request 45 minutes after the second request - finally someone came over. They explained that the lamb goes quickly so they kept running out before they got to us. OK, fine, but then why not stop at our table first after we asked? But, we got skipped a lot. There was a time where 8 of us had our coasters turned over for well over 20 minutes without anyone coming over.

    We also ordered sodas, cocktails, etc. My first Coke was out right away. My second one took 30 minutes. Boyfriend asked a server if they had a particular type of hard cider - she left to check and when she came back 20 minutes later, she had forgotten to check. So he said nevermind, and ordered a different drink. That also came roughly 30 minutes later.

    So, overall, the service was extremely slow. I realize we were a large party but whether it's 3 parties of 4, or one party of 12, I don't think the service should be any different. We had two people serving us and neither felt faster than the other. They were extremely friendly and nice people, but it was rather crazy to have to wait so long for meat servings, and 30 minutes for drinks.

    It also took 30 minutes to get the checks, as we were all paying separately. There was an automatic 18% gratuity - which actually means they shorted themselves because we all agreed 20% would've been the norm. My boyfriend and I were charged $180 after gratuity. Sodas were $3.25 a piece - not free refills, apparently.

    Anyway, I might give it another try another day when it's just a few friends or just a date with my boyfriend, but I wasn't thrilled about the slow service last night. It was a Tuesday night, too, so I can't imagine what it's like on a Friday or Saturday. The food was yummy, but I certainly would've rather paid $90 to eat at a 'normal' fine-dining restaurant where I didn't have to practically beg to get my food..

Specialties

We are an authentic Churrascaria (Brazilian Steakhouse) from Rio Grande do Sul in southern Brazil which features superior service, unrivaled cuisine and a unique dining experience.

You will be pampered by our authentic Gaucho chefs who come to your tableside to carve a variety of succulent meats right off their skewers.  Our featured meats have been slow-roasted over an open flame and lightly seasoned to preserve the unique taste of each tender cut.

Adding to our unique dining experience, you will be treated to a sumptuous, gourmet salad and vegetable bar featuring over 30 items including imported cheeses, cured meats, fresh vegetables and more.  We complement your meal with traditional Brazilian side dishes such as caramelized bananas, crispy polenta, seasoned mashed potatoes and our world-famous cheese bread (pao de queijo).

No meal is complete without trying a refreshing caipirinha, something from our award-winning wine list or one of our decadent desserts.
