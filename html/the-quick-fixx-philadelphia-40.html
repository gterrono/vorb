    My first visit to the Quick Fixx was probably about 2-3 stars. I called in an order for the Seasonal Goat Cheese salad, and it was delivered quickly. The delivery guy was also really nice and didn't seem to mind the production of me trying to get my food and pay while the puppy I was dog-sitting attempted to escape. When I finally got my salad, I was a little disappointed. All of the toppings listed sounded wonderful and actually tasted very fresh, but there was verrry little of each one.     I had a living social takeout deal and decided to give the Quick Fixx a second try. I ordered the arugula salad (with olive oil, lemon and parmesan, $5) and added grilled shrimp for another $5. I also threw in bruschetta with tuscan white bean spread ($4) in case the salad wasn't filling enough. I picked up my order and it was ready within the promised time, about 15 minutes. The salad could have been a little bigger in my opinion, since it was just arugula, but I didn't feel ripped off. The dressing was well balanced, but there was a little too much black pepper on the salad and I would have liked a little more parmesan. The grilled shrimp however, were SO good. Perfectly cooked. I could have eaten 30 of them, in addition to the 5 they gave me. The bread for the bruschetta was nicely grilled and brushed with olive oil. The white bean spread was ok, but didn't have too much flavor.

    All together my order came to $15, which is about twice what I usually like to spend on lunch. I definitely would get this for dinner if the portions were slightly bigger.

Specialties

Chef-Inspired Takeout....quality food at convenient prices.

We offer fresh, cook-to-order pastas, grilled artisanal flatbreads, and tasty salads.  Other specialties include hand breaded mozzarella sticks and fried calamari as well as our famous "Panzarotti Poppers" (fried goat cheese).

Ask about our two daily discounted menu options:
"Late Night Fixx" - (happy hour pricing after 10pm)
"10 Spot Menu" - ($10 cash-only lunch specials)

Looking to cater an office lunch?  Our menu offers a great alternative from your normal choices.

Meet the Business Owner

Matt L. Business Owner

Being someone who has spent their entire working career in the hospitality industry, customer service has always been my number one priority.  From day one, we have actively sought to hire only people who possess the skill set of being genuine and friendly.  All other job skills can be taught, but finding someone who truly cares about being kind and helpful comes from within.  Our service separates us from our competition, but it is our product that keeps our loyal patrons coming back.

The QUICK FIXX concept is built around the idea of "Chef-Inspired Takeout." Serving good food, at a good value, with a friendly attitude is a simple mantra, but one we take very seriously.  It is the job of our Executive Chef and his culinary team to ensure we are meeting this mission each and every day on each and every order.    If you are ever dissatisfied with any aspect or your experience, please let us know, so that we may remedy your situation.  After all, candid feedback is how we get better.
