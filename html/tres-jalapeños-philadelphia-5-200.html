    $12.95 unlimited tacos on Tuesdays, say whattt. I've lived in South Philly all my life and had to check it out. The unlimited special included crunchy (hard shell), traditional (corn tortilla), and california (flour tortilla) in chicken, pork, ground beef, or steak options. 3 tacos to an order so you can try all of them if your stomach allows or if you come with a few friends.

    The food was good, but nothing extraordinary that makes it stand out. The unlimited deal makes it worth it though. They took like 15 minutes to come out so definitely request the next round of tacos once you get the first order. I had a margarita and it tasted too much like salt water to even drink (not sure if this is the "traditional" style, but I'm used to sweet/fruity margaritas with salt rims so I'm not basing my review on it. Just an FYI if you want to order it though).

    What made the experience bad was the horrible service. So there's a waitress and a girl who refills water. My friend's water sat empty for 20+ minutes and he didn't get a refill until he tracked the refill girl down. The waitress was friendly and willing to give us extra salsa verde, etc when we asked. However, after paying, we waited for our change for almost 30 minutes. Getting fed up, we walked up to the counter and requested the change ourselves. There were about 5 groups of customers eating so it wasn't super packed either.

    3 stars for food
    1 star for the super affordable unlimited taco deal
    -2 stars for service

Specialties

Come join us for margarita Monday (we are now a full bar) .. Our Monday special on margaritas buy one get one free and our Tuesday special taco Tuesday all you can eat tacos. Try our fresh homemade guacamole  made with fresh avocado, onions and cilantro, with a squirt of lime juice and just the right amount of seasonings. Its to die for. Try it with our fresh fried tortillas chips along with the homemade red salsa with dices of onion and cilantro, seasoned perfect. Our business specializes in the burritos.. They are even tastier when drenched with our homemade salsa Verde our red sauce our amazing sweet and spicy mole sauce, then baked with cheese on top to melted perfection. Or try out our enchiladas, your choice of meat wrapped in a tortilla smothered in our homemade salsas. We also offer a variety of VEGETARIAN meals made with our fresh cooked organic beans. All our food is made fresh from scratch everyday.

History

Established in 2006.

The restaurant first started as Los Jalapenos that was located on 4th and Moore. We were there for 5 years then moves to our new  location 901 s 8th st. When i came from Mexico its was what I always wanted to do was cook Tex-Mex food. I have been in the Mexican food business for 18+ years.

Meet the Business Owner

Jaime t. Business Owner

I Jaime Tapia am a native from Mexico. I entered when i was 17 years of age. I started to work in the restaurant business. And realized it was my passion to cook and prepare food for the Mexican food lovers.  I have been in the food industry for 18 years. And i enjoy my passion as an owner and cook of my restaurant.
