    This cozy, hoppin' South Philly cantina makes the BEST margaritas I've ever had.

    The menu here instigates the battle between my eyes & my stomach--I want to order way more than I can really handle, and so my only realistic option is to keep coming back here. Two visits in, I've had their amazing guacamole, several pitchers of margaritas on the rocks with salt, chips with green and red salsas, and burritos 2 ways: the verduras, and the shrimp; both loaded up with a la carte add-ons like jalapenos, sour cream, and guac... I prefer the shrimp burrito, but the verduras is quite good. This is one of many items on the vegan- and vegetarian-friendly menu.

    Their board specials vie for my attention, particularly the ceviches. Their desserts beckon, but I have not yet figured out how to get them in ma belly.  

    Service is great; very attentive. The teamwork on the staff appears pretty tight. This is a hipster haven, but caters to a diverse crowd of all ages and types.

    When the Cantina first opened, I was new to South Philly and found that it was a great place for excellent vegetarian and vegan food and delicious margaritas... a fine spot for all occasions.

    However, four years later I no longer truly enjoy coming to the Cantina. Perhaps it's because I'm getting older and more crotchety and losing patience with hipsters. Perhaps it's because I know there are better places to go within a couple blocks. Perhaps the quality of the food has gone down. Perhaps it is darker and noisier now than it was four years ago. Perhaps I have just gotten sick of going there. It could be any of these things.

    If you decide you want to go, and you are more than welcome to make that choice, here are some tips.

    -Because the restaurant can be so dark inside, I recommend going during the day so you can actually read your menu. During that time it tends to be less crowded too, which is an added bonus.
    -The House margarita is great, and so are the flavored margs, particularly the blood orange.
    -As far as food, I have enjoyed their burrito with vegan beef. Also their nachos, which you can get with vegan beef as well.
    -They always have a ton of specials and mark which ones are vegetarian/vegan.
