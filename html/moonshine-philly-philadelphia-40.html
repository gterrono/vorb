Specialties

Moonshine Philly has quickly become South Philadelphia's favorite bar and restaurant. Whether you're a long-time local or you're new to the neighborhood, you'll appreciate our tasty menu and impressive drink selection. Our food is made fresh daily, featuring classic dishes as well as homemade specialties developed from our own recipes. From our Cheeseburger Eggroll and Moonshine Mac and Cheese to Blackened Chicken Pesto Gnocchi and Falafel Sliders, there's truly something for everyone at Moonshine Philly.

In addition to our delicious food, we also have a full bar serving up specialty drinks, bottled and canned beer and craft brews. In fact, we scour the country for great beer and have a constantly-rotating menu of 10 different craft beers on draft. Whether you're in the mood for a great meal or a fun night of drinking with friends, Moonshine Philly is the perfect option!

History

Established in 2013.

Moonshine Philly was transformed out of the old East Side Saloon, which stood for years as a local dive bar. We opened in 2013, and our mission has always been to offer delicious food, unique beer and a friendly atmosphere for our customers to enjoy. A recent addition to South Philadelphia, we look forward to serving our friends and neighbors for many years to come.

Meet the Business Owner

Richard F. Business Owner

Richard has always been in the restaurant industry, and was excited to open Moonshine Philly in an up-and-coming neighborhood. He loves the growth the area has seen over the past couple of years, and is excited to be part of its revitalization. Richard hopes your visit to Moonshine Philly is so enjoyable that you quickly become one of our regulars.
