    "How is this place?"

    If you are ever asked this question, the only appropriate answer is: "Are you serious? You've never been here?"

    Let's face it. Breakfast options are pretty slim in Olde City, and if you're perusing around the area around 1030ish in the morning, you either have to wait a half hour for a cheesesteak or "breakfast" beer at one of the bars that tend to have more customers after 11:37pm on a Saturday night.

    Breakfast/Brunch here is a good one, and the homemade (and fresh!) Kaiser roll is reason alone to come back. I've been here twice, and tried two breakfast sandwiches which reign supreme in all of the breakfast sandwiches I have ever tried. I've happily tasted The Forager (Seared King Oyster Mushroom, Braised, Kale, Farm Egg, Green Meadow Young Swiss, Black Trumpet Mayo) and The Hickory Town (Lancaster Bologna, Farm Egg, Amish Horseradish Cheddar, Gherkin Mayo, Fried Red Onions). My goodness, both were delicious! Hearty, flavorful, and just enough "stuffing" so it doesn't fall out.

    Coffee is decent and strong, and the danish is the perfect sweet ending.

    Say yes to High Street!
