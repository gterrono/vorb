    Listed in Oscar Wilde, Vegetarians Unite!, Best Places I've had Beer!, Motown Philly Back Again

    The Good Dog Bar is rated 5 stars in my book for being a really f'n cool dive bar. Again, the 5 stars are for it being a dive bar so don't think you're coming in for a 4-course meal. This is just a really f'n cool bar!

    Hubs and I read that it was a Guy Fieri feature on DDD so we had to give it a try. When we walked in, it was dark and sort of dingy but in a really cool vibe sort of way. We bellied up to the bar hoping to score some beer, and a late lunch. Our server, who could be Sarah Silverman's sister, was on her game from the start. She coached us through some of the local beers, offering us a taste of some that we were interested in, and then walked us through the menu. They have a plethora of great vegan options, which makes going for something vegetarian pretty easy. I opted for the vegan burger but added some gouda and grilled onions. It was served with beet chips and some fries that were a mixture of potatoes and sweet potatoes.  It was TO DIE FOR!

    There were plenty of bar seats, booths, and a few tables. There are also lots of TVs available. Sarah Silverman gave us some ideas for some weekend events going on in the area, and was super nice welcoming us to the area. LOVED the Good Dog Bar.

    I actually found myself here as a result of a contingency plan.  I planned on going to another bar that was known for having one of the best burgers in the city according to Glen Macnow's Hamburger Hunt as well as plenty of word of mouth.  Needless to say, I was quite anxious to try it.  I won't name the place because I was quite disappointed in not being able to eat there due to a 90 minute wait and not offering reservations.  I remembered Good Dog also being on the list so I decided to give them a try.  

    I got their Good Dog burger which has carmelized onions and roquefort cheese.  I wasn't sure what roquefort cheese was, but it sounds sophisticated so it had to be good.  I learned it was a fancy type of blue cheese, which I'm not a big fan of, but this was better.  It's not as strong of a taste, but complimented the burger nicely.  Oh, and the burger is made with the cheese inside the patty.  What a nice surprise as it comes oozing out at every bite!  As for the rest of the burger, I ordered it medium.  It wasn't very big, but it was certainly thick.  It fit on the soft brioche bun like they were made for each other.  Finally, the onions gave it a nice taste to top it all off with.  As if the burger wasn't delicious enough, the fries it came with were amazing.  They fry it in duck fat, which makes for a great way to cook fries with.  After my meal, I was no longer upset about not making it to that other place.  Sometimes in dining when one door closes due to a long wait time, another one opens.
