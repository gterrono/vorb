    Unique little cafe on Market street in Old Town. Lots of unique options for sandwiches, salads, and meat/cheese boards.

    I opted for the duck meatball sandwich, which was slightly sweeter than your typical Meatball sandwich along with a great balance of bread-to-meat.

    Service was very friendly and efficient. It's a small spot, so be prepared to sit near/with someone not in your party.

    Only negative is that it is pretty expensive for relatively small portions. For high quality, unique food, I'm willing to pay for it though.

    I have eaten at a lot of great restaurants in Philly this year and I can say without a doubt that the meal I had at High Street was the best yet. We went with the chef's menu and everything was so carefully thought out and executed that I just couldn't wait to see what came next.

    The service was thoughtful and unassuming and the ambiance relaxed. The food did all if the talking.

    Usually I look to more exotic cuisines for exciting meals but there was nothing boring about what was served. The flavors and ingredient combinations were the work of a truly gifted chef. I can't wait to go again!
