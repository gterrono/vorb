        I should start by saying that I might be a little biased towards this place because I ABSOLUTELY LOVE IT!         I've been to HipCityVeg a couple times. Each time, I left satisfied.

        Let's start with the Groothie. This smoothie is enough to fill you up. It's made with apple, pineapple, banana and either kale or spinach. It's a great option if you are looking for a refreshing and sweeter beverage/meal replacement.

        The Ziggy Burger made me question if I was actually eating meat. It was juicy and flavorful. I was shocked a vegan burger could be this good. My only complaint is it got a bit greasy towards the end, but that makes you feel like you are eating a "real" burger.

        The tofu curry wrap has a good flavor, but the wrap was overwhelming the filling. The bread itself was also nothing spectacular. I wish it was a tomato or spinach wrap but it was kind of old whole wheat. The sauces that come with it definitely increase the flavor profile a couple notches. They are a must. It was decent. If there was different bread and more filling, it could be something really spectacular.

        You can't forget the sweet potato fries. The best part is the cilantro bean sauce that comes with them. The fries themselves are decent. They are the consistency of normal fast food french fries. (I prefer thicker steak fries, but these are good). The bean sauce is definitely the star of this dish! It's sweet and tangy goodness makes you coming back for more.

        Lastly, the banana whips! These are a perfect option for those not wanting to down all the calories of ice cream.

        The following reasons is why HipCityVeg only earns three stars:

        1.) The price. It's a little bit expensive. (I could easily make a banana whip at home for an eighth of the price

        2.) The size. I wish there was more of a seating area. This place is more of a grab and go. You are lucky it you could get a spot at one of the 3 tables for 2. Additionally, everyone is crammed in there to order. It can get a little claustrophobic. Bathrooms would also be a nice touch.

        3.) The food is alright. It's a nice option if you are looking for something quick. I find it to be a little inconsistent with flavoring though.

        Regardless I know when a Ziggy Burger craving hits, I will happily make my way to Rittenhouse. I also have been meaning to try their Udon Noodle Salad.
