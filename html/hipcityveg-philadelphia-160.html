    Love this place, 100% vegan /veggie, and earth friendly recycled materials! And the food here is top notch.  It's cramped and small, so get take-out because there's only 3 small tables to dine-in.

    I had the Ziggy burger ($8.5), pretty much like a loaded veggie burger with all the toppings and sauce.  I think it was the best veggie burger i ever had in my life.  It was huge and plump, and oozing with juice and sauces.  Delicious!   I've also tried the taco salad ($8?), the cilantro dressing was amazing, and the sweet potato fries ($4) were also amazing.  I have also tried the Curry Tofu Wrap, this was also delicious, loaded with juicy tofu in a sweet curry sauce, with avacado, lettuce, and sprouts.  The cucumber dipping sauce is also yummy, although not needed since the wrap is juicy enough by itself!

    They have this green health smoothie shake for $5.  It tastes soooo good and is loaded with fruits & veggies.  Called the "Groothie".. Order it!  Yumm

    I would eat here every day if I could afford a $15 lunch every day!  But, for now. I will highly recommend this place, and I'll keep coming back (maybe once a month lol).  I wish they would give you discounted fries with purchase of a sanwhich, like a "combo" option, or have a frequent buyer's card, but I guess you can use Level Up for that.  Best vegetarian take-out in the city hands down.
