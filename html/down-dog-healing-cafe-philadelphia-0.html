        I have been trying to make my way to DDHC since it opened!  Finally, the stars aligned this weekend as my husband and I were in the neighborhood on our way to have Pho for lunch!  We popped into DDHC with the intention of grabbing a tea to go.  Everything about this place was so cool - that it totally changed our afternoon plans and we ended up foregoing our Pho lunch, and grabbing a drink and a bite to eat at DDHC instead.

        The owner sorta gave me an around the world taste of the various teas on the roster - they were all very different, but equally delicious, that I found myself saying "I think this one is my favorite" on several occasions!  

        Then I got around to the almond date smoothie - which was so fresh and tasty, that it nearly changed my life!  The owner said that this drink would be on the menu year around - and that it would possibly be served warm in the winter!  Now, I'm super excited about the prospects of trying it warm on a wintery Philly evening!  

        The owner was very patient / knowledgeable and explained the Ayurvedic principles that guided her business model! With such reasonable prices and hours from 7AM to 7PM, I will certainly be back in the near future!

        I stumbled upon this cute little cafe at the perfect moment - right as my blood sugar levels were crashing. Upon entering, my inner yogi delighted in the laid back, earthy vibe, the small drink and snackish menu offerings - and who doesn't love the name? I was immediately greeted by the lone staffer behind the counter, who offered to answer any questions that I had before returning to the conversation two other patrons were having with her. As much as I wanted to try one of their healthy, healing drinks, the prices were just a bit...much, especially since I can make those myself. I decided instead to indulge (and check out) their vegan, gluten free goods and nabbed a GF oatmeal cookie for later, and three of the little date-coconut balls for an instant, healthy, snack to get me back home.

        The date balls, although massively expensive at $2 each, were delicious and very satisfying. The cookie was also tasty, crunchy-chewy in texture with a nice spiced oats flavor - not bad, and certainly would confuse anyone who didn't know it was gluten free. I'll have to come back to this neighborhood spot for a tonic and to chill in their adorable space!

        I appreciate the value that Down Dog adds -improving customers' health by providing nourishing food and drinks.
        After having a very poor nights sleep, I gained restoration with a warming cup of kitchari and green detox soup -perfect for my Vata constitution! I really appreciated the kind staff who were helpful in navigating me through the menu and informing me of Ayurveda.
        I love that Down Dog has integrity and soul. Most eateries seem to sell food devoid of nutrients, prepared as cheaply as possible that wreck havoc on our bodies. Down Dog centers it's menu on healthful, wholesome food. I often experience indigestion and an inflamed, irritated stomach when dining out. My belly was left feeling happy after my meal at Down Dog thanks to their judicious use of salt and fat.
        The atmosphere is centering and calming. I generally feel uncomfortable dining at loud eateries bustling with crowds. The Sunday morning that I visited Down Dog was quiet and enjoyable. I was able to take the time to relax and fully appreciate my meal and tea. The wi-fi was a bonus too, giving me the opportunity to extend my time at the cafe and complete a little bit of work.
        I wish I lived closer to Down Dog so I could frequent it more often.

      Specialties

      Down Dog Healing Cafe offers seasonally balancing teas, tonics and elixirs so that you may live more in tune with nature.  The seasonal menu is inspired by Ayurveda as a means of prevention as well as to treat imbalances in the body.  The cafe is presented in a "coffee shop" environment offering a comfortable place to meet friends or hook-up to our complimentary wi-fi.  We take our ingredients very seriously.  We use locally grown, chemical free, gmo-free, fresh ingredients.  We offer gluten-free, vegan, vegetarian & raw options when in tune with the seasons.
