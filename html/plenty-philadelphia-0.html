    Plenty is a new concept for the neighborhood, or at least for the old guard who are still here, and… Plenty is a new concept for the neighborhood, or at least for the old guard who are still here, and some of whom struggle with the gentrification of the community and being inundating by crowds of out of area visitors. As a foodie, I think there is room for all as long as the food is great and the people serving you friendly, respectful of all and attentive.
