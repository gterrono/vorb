    We tried Mixto for brunch this past Sunday after dining partner went exploring on yelp, discovered and quickly began obsessing over their croissant french toast. He seemed to enjoy it, as his comments ranged from "It tastes fluffy" to "It's so fluffy". I was considering the Argentinian Breakfast. However, I wasn't very hungry and decided to go on the lighter side with American breakfast of two eggs your way, bacon, and roasted potatoes. Can't wait to come back for dinner and another brunch!

    My wife and I love this place for a few very good reasons. First, the food is very good Latin American fusion with a wide variety of elements from several countries in the Caribbean, Central and South America. It's also one of the few places where I can get a cold Presidente, which is the national beer of the Dominican Republic and a decent pilsner - which is always refreshing during the summer. While their drinks can be pricey, their mojitos are tasty and are a great accompaniment to their lighter foods and appetizers.

    The most important reason why this place is special to us is that it was the setting for our first date and we now get to take our lovely daughter their for brunches. She's our little Mixto legacy. ¡Buen Provecho y Ma'a Salaama!
