    Listed in The Squid's Picks

    I was invited to a function here a few years back. It's nestled right on Front Street between Market & Chestnut. It's hardly a location one would describe as hidden but in all honesty,  I don't know that I would have discovered it on my own.

    I've only eaten in the dining room once, during the aforementioned function. All other visits, I've dined in the bar. The bar is a decent size and there are tables in the bar area as well. It's clean and I've rarely found it crowded.

    I've had several items from their menu. Grilled salmon, crab ravioli, gnocchi, four cheese ravioli, homemade sausage & a variety of their salads and apps. This is Italian cuisine at its finest. Ummm...two words that come to mind when I think about Spasso are: SUPERB & AWESOME.  

    The ambiance in the bar is super cozy. I've always been treated extremely well by the friendly bartenders/waitstaff. It's a great place to go on a date. I even enjoy going here alone.

    I've seen some reviews state that the kitchen was slow. I've never witnessed that personally in the times I've been there....but even if it was, for the quality of food they are serving - I would be happy to wait a little longer for it.

    Special note: I am a FREAK for their grilled calamari salad. Ordering it when I go here is beyond my control, it's compulsory. I highly recommend it.

    As I type this I find myself swept away into a daydream where I wander into Spasso, sit at the bar, order a grilled calamari salad (or two), a side of broccoli rabe and a few beers. Ah...pure bliss.

    I love this place and can't wait to go back.
