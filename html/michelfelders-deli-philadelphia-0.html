    Love this place. Love, love, love it. I know you're thinking... "it's a deli - whoop-dee-do!" Check the crowd at lunchtime and you'll see I am not the only one who thinks it's one of the better ones in town. The rolls are fresh and piled high with delicious Boar's Head meat. Sandwiches are made to order and they have a serious system down for cranking them out. There may be a line but it *really* moves.

    They also offer salads made to order - Saladworks style without as much variety or cost. You get an entire garden worth topped with pasta, turkey, egg and a roll for under $7.00 (they have other options too). This salad ends up being a pound or more every time I pick one up.  

    Admittedly, it is more or less just a deli but the sandwiches are delicious and the salads are huge and inexpensive!

    Listed in Market East/Chinatown

    This is one of the few places I will get food at in the Gallery.

    Since I work in the Market East part of the city I have to scour around to find fresh food that isn't deep fried. This is a great spot for affordable sandwiches and salads when I don't feel like venturing outside during the cold weather.

    If you are going to go with a sandwich they use Boars Head meats and cheeses. Their salads are fresh and plentifull. Like Colleen said, you can tell that it's a popular lunch spot by the long line. But don't pass it up, the service is super fast!

    Note: CASH ONLY!
