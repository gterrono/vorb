    If you like local coffee that tastes batches better than that which shall not be named corporate coffee shop.... And you want a choice of nondairy milk in your espresso drink or just your coffee, you will be floored by coming here. They have coconut, almond, soy and then creamer in 4 containers. They also have natural less processed sugars and raw blue agave - and you don't have to ask.

    However. Be careful if you would like your drink made with unsweetened nondairy milk- just like most places it is sweetened here.  It's not a big deal though cause it's so darned good with the extra sweetness. If you want the real bean flavor, be a purist and get black coffee or espresso.

    The food is paired perfectly to match their coffee and tea beverage focus. Sandwiches, bagelwiches and dessert. For a coffeehouse, it's pretty perfect. Everything is vegan- so that is a problem because it makes me want one of everything.  The sandwiches have fresh spinach, tomatoes, meat substitute, hummus and oh yes, vegan mayo, vegan creme cheese and of course things like pb.  They even have gluten free cake and bagels!!  Heaven in a coffeehouse would probably look like this.

    The staff rocks. This place rocks.  Please bring one to very major city in the US, and then maybe the world. Thanks.
