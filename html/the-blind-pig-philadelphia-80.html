        A group of friends and I stopped in at The Blind Pig to fuel up before a beer festival this past Saturday.

        We walked in pretty much right as they opened, and it looked like they weren't fully set up yet. I've worked in the restaurant business before, so I know that sometimes this happens and it really didn't bother me.

        When our server came over, she asked if we had been there before (why doesn't this happen more often?). When we replied that we had not, she took the time to explain each item on the specials board for us. I really appreciated this.

        To start, we went with the poutine to share, which was delicious. The fries and gravy were beautifully seasoned, and it really gave the creamy cheese curds an opportunity to shine. For something so simple, it was very well-executed.

        For my entree, I tried the gator sausage sandwich from the specials board. The sausage itself was really great. The flavors were very balanced, and it had that great natural casing snap that really lets you know it's legit. My only complaint would be that I think there was just a little too much mustard on the sandwich. At times it really overpowered the flavor of the sausage, which was a shame. Next time I'll probably get the mustard on the side.

        Also, because we got the poutine as an appetizer, the server suggested I swap my side of fries for a side salad, which was a great idea. The salad was really tasty. It wasn't over or under dressed, and the dressing itself was mild in flavor, which I liked because I actually got to taste the salad itself.

        Hubby had the lamb sliders, which I had an opportunity to try. They were really delicious. I would have never thought to try smoked paprika with lamb, but that smoked paprika aioli was just perfect for those yummy little burgers. I will probably get that next time I go. Our friends had the pulled pork sandwich (he said it was good, but not amazing) and the Cubano (he really liked it).

        All in all, it was a great experience. We let our server know that we were heading to the beer festival, so she made sure we made it out in time as well. I will definitely be back to try more of their awesome specials.
