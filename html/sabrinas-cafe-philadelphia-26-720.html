    Oh Sabrina, why must you only be in Philly???? Why can't you move to Texas??? Please, oh pretty please???

    Ok enough begging. Lets talk food.

    We had been to the location on Callowhill - actually right before Hurricane Sandy, but that's another story. Anyway, I have fond memories of Sabrina's so we decided to go to the one in the Italian Market.

    Everything was just as I remembered: delicious. We both had breakfast sandwiches which were made to order and cooked perfectly. They do have glutten free bread if need be. The turkey bacon is the best that I have ever had. We scored a table on the sidewalk so our morning was perfect. It is a BYOB so we were seated around some healthcare workers who had just come off shift & were ready to get their drink on.

    The staff was helpful and friends. The food phenomenal. Go. You will not regret either location.
