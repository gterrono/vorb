    Listed in Best of 2013, Home Town Favorites

    All of the hype and rumors I have been hearing about this place finally came to fruition during a recent day trip to Philadelphia!  My sister went to school in Philly and has been talking about the roast pork sandwich from DiNic's since she was a freshman.  Naturally, I had to try it.

    The place was not hard to find.  Once inside Reading Terminal Market, I just looked for the area with the most people waiting in line.  The line itself wasn't terribly long when I got in it, and they were moving customers through fairly quickly.  I was greeted with a weird act of kindness not normally found in Philadelphia, so that was a nice touch.  After ordering, it only took about five minutes to pay and get my sandwich in my hand.  There were plenty of tables and counter space to dine at, and of course there was great people watching from the tables.

    After my first bite into the sandwich, I knew why humans evolved the ability to use tools, to make this delicious food!  What Brennan Hough is to singing, DiNic's roast pork with provolone and broccoli rabe is to sandwiches (a mix between Fergie and Jesus, only Fergie is the best sandwich you have ever eaten).  Literally everything about this sandwich was perfect.  Just when I thought each bite couldn't get any better, I took the next... until there were no more bites.  The flavors of the pork, cheese, and greens all complemented each other perfectly.  The textures of the bread and innards ranged from slightly crunchy to melt-in-your mouth smooth.  I will never forget this eating experience and I would hate myself if I ever make it back to Philadelphia and do not stop at DiNic's.
