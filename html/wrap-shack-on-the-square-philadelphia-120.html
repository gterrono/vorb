        Long ago, I had one of their burritos and was not terribly impressed.  Then, on a recent weekend I was back here with a friend who wanted a burrito, so I figured I'd get one, too.  

        There is a large selection of burritos to try, both meat and vegetarian.  You can eat in -- at the bar or a table -- or get it to go.  I got a fajita burrito in a spinach tortilla for eating in nearby Rittenhouse.  It was tasty, with a good balance of meat, veggies, rice, beans, and sauce; the wrap was heated to have a nice hint of crunch.

        The orders were cooked pretty quickly.  The bar tenders were friendly.  Prices were reasonable (about $8 for a burrito).
