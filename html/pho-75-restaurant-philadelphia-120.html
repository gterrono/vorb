    Listed in 2014 Yelp 100 Challenge, The City of Brotherly Love

    First Pho place in Philly that I will say is good.  (And I've had my fair share of pho on both coasts)
    Broth isn't as flavorful as I hoped it would be, but the noodles were perfect and the portion of the meats, tripe, etc were up to par.

    THEY ONLY SERVE PHO.  You will not find the rice or vermicelli noodle dishes here.

    Service is super fast.  Standard side additions come with pho: limes, basil, sprouts.

    Spacious dining area with the tradition viet decor and vibe - nice and casual.

    Parking is tough on the weekends so come with patience.

    There are other Viet restaurants within the same shopping complex which I have yet to try and compare to this place to. Stay tuned..

    Perfect Pho. As a great fan of this, most perfect of soups, I have to say that this one was exceptional. It was on Anthony Bourdains list, so we decided to pop in - yet to be disappointed by one of his picks and this was no exception. Pure dining room atmosphere, simple, bare bones menu with the one thing they have perfected - pho. I personally like the brisket, round mix, because the fat from the briskest enriches the broth just enough to make it a solid 10. Drizzle in some siracha and voila!

    Love this place. I first came here when I was feeling really sick and craving comfort food. Even with my senses dulled by the worst cold ever, the delicious and complex broth knocked me off my feet.

    We recently timed how long it took for us to receive our food after ordering - I kid you not, it took 1 min and 37 seconds. Get the drip coffee (with condensed milk) for a cute treat (and powerful pick-me-up) to enjoy after your meal.

    The portions are sizeable and prices totally reasonable. Try to go on weekdays when parking a is a bit more manageable.
