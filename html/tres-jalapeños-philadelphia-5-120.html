    This is a delivery review, as I have never been there.

    Have had delivery from Tres Jalapenos a few times now, and I can say it is pretty good. Something more authentic than The Cantina yet less so than, say, the taco truck at 4th and Washington. I do appreciate being able to be understood in English when I call.

    The food is damn good. The green enchiladas are no joke, my Yelp friends. They have more than a little kick to them. It is a great mix of cheesy, doughy and spicy.

    So, yeah, feeling like Mexican but dont wanna get off the couch? Tres Jalapenos is a good option, albeit a tad pricey.

Specialties

Come join us for margarita Monday (we are now a full bar) .. Our Monday special on margaritas buy one get one free and our Tuesday special taco Tuesday all you can eat tacos. Try our fresh homemade guacamole  made with fresh avocado, onions and cilantro, with a squirt of lime juice and just the right amount of seasonings. Its to die for. Try it with our fresh fried tortillas chips along with the homemade red salsa with dices of onion and cilantro, seasoned perfect. Our business specializes in the burritos.. They are even tastier when drenched with our homemade salsa Verde our red sauce our amazing sweet and spicy mole sauce, then baked with cheese on top to melted perfection. Or try out our enchiladas, your choice of meat wrapped in a tortilla smothered in our homemade salsas. We also offer a variety of VEGETARIAN meals made with our fresh cooked organic beans. All our food is made fresh from scratch everyday.

History

Established in 2006.

The restaurant first started as Los Jalapenos that was located on 4th and Moore. We were there for 5 years then moves to our new  location 901 s 8th st. When i came from Mexico its was what I always wanted to do was cook Tex-Mex food. I have been in the Mexican food business for 18+ years.
