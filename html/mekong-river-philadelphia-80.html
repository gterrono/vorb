        This is the week of dining with work colleagues and checking out new places.

        Every time I think I have tried every Pho spot to be tried in Philly,  I always miss one !  

        This place is LEGIT.

        Got the Pho Dac Biet.   Sometimes I just want the deluxe Pho for all its flavors and my "benchmark" for a good restaurant serving this is if the meatballs are actually edible!   These were fantastic.  Broth was SO flavorful.

        And,

        The other benchmark is the coffee.  Cafe Su Da.   This was HEAVEN.

        And,  something that is a rare find in good Vietnamese restaurants ...
        -- a clean bathroom !   It has been my experience unfortunately !   This bathroom is huge and clean and lovely !

        Servers totally friendly,  made recommendations and food came out fast and piping hot.

        Will absolutely be back.

        The food here is alright. Big portions, but however some of the food lacks. It doesn't lack much though. The best part of this spot is the interior design of the restaurant. It is clean and relatively very easy to spot the restaurant since it's the only one of its kind on the block. They try to do a Cambodian and Vietnamese themed restaurant and it shows. I got the luc lac (lok lak) with rice and the meat was very good. However, the lime black pepper dressing was very salty. When I say salty, I mean VERY salty.

        Try the spot out for yourself.
