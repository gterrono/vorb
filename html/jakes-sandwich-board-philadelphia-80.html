    Jake's impresses me a little more each and every trip. In my personal opinion it is clawing for best sandwich shop in Philly. Think about any classic Philadelphia sandwich and just add a frill. In no means is it over the top though. They celebrate Sriraccha Sauce here, they even have "I Love Sriraccha " T-shirts for sale.  

    They have a great cheesesteak here called the 'Firesteak.' It is a traditional cheesesteak with a deep fried long hot on a Carangi roll and for a small up charge you can get ghost pepper on it. If you choose the ghost pepper, you must sign a waiver. Pork is their speciality though. The 50/50 sandwich is the crown jewel of Jake's, at least in my opinion. It's half bacon, half roast pork, Sriraccha spread and sharp provolone.

    They must buy a fresh cooked pig everyday because there is a full pig on display everyday that they get the pork from.
