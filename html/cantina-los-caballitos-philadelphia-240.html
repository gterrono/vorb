    This is probably my favorite place in South Philly. Whether you're going there for dinner with friends, cocktails after work, or to party on a Saturday night, Cantina never disappoints.

    Their staff is cool and friendly, their bartenders mix a mean drink, and their kitchen dishes out really delicious fajitas, burritos, nachos... you name it! The bacon-wrapped scallops and fried plantains are definitely some of the most delicious things I have ever tasted. On weeknights they usually play great movies on the TV behind the bar (Top Gun, My Cousin Vinnie... the list goes on) and the people hanging out for drinks on Monday and Tuesday nights are usually good to talk to and interesting. It's definitely a place you can go for a drink and conversation on your own.

    Add to all of this their abundant outdoor seating in the summer months and the Tacate-and-Tequila citywide specials and Cantina is definitely a winner. Not the least-expensive Mexican restaurant on the block but you definitely get what you pay for!

    We were seated/greeted right away and had chips and salsa within about a minute of sitting down. The service was great. Our waiter was attentive without being annoying.

    It was great. I was expecting, hoping, wishing, for a California style burrito that I had been so craving and this delivered to the T.
