        I'm a little surprised I never reviewed Shake Shack before, since I've been here a few times and I'm absolutely in love with it. No other burger is as perfect looking and as mouthwatering as the Shack Stack.

        After coming home from a trip to LA and Vegas, dejected and miserable because I was unable to get In-N-Out, I sought out Shake Shack for solace. I'm not really sad I missed In-N-Out anymore, because no one can be sad while eating a Shake Shack burger.

        The burgers here are delicious! I would love to get my hands on the meat ratio they use, because their patty is meaty, juicy, and just fatty enough. They are consistent in their use of perfect looking ingredients - center cut slices of bright red, perfectly ripe tomatoes, and beautiful, crisp green lettuce leaves - not that sad white/light green crap they have at McD's so often. And what else to sandwich all this perfection than in a lightly toasted Martin's potato bun? (I read one review that said Shake Shack doesn't toast their buns - but this is wrong. They toast the insides lightly! For sure!).

        Definitely the best thing about Shake Shack is their consistency. I feel that I have had the same, identical, delicious burger the first time I came here (a year or two ago) as I did when I came here last (last week). The Shack Stack burger (with the crispy, cheese filled portobello mushroom) is to die for! Just wait a little before digging in, because the cheese is seriously very hot, and you don't want a burnt tongue when you have something truly awesome to taste.

        I am not a fan of crinkle cut french fries, but Shake Shack fries don't do any harm. Not bad, but nothing to write home about. It is the standard accompaniment and I will always order them alongside my burger.

        For dessert, the Liberty Shell concrete is perfect! I love cannoli's, so I was ecstatic to try it out in ice-cream form. I wish they would add a bit more of the cannoli shells (Termini bro's!), but you don't really have to fix what isn't broken, I guess.

        The service here - although not a wait staff - is actually pretty awesome. They offer to take away your trash/tray once you're done eating, and the cashiers are always friendly. Maybe sometimes too enthusiastic at times - one time they chanted my name loudly as they called my order and proudly announced that I was their like 136th customer that day. I was confused yet amused.

        I would rate this place 5 stars if the price were a tad bit more agreeable, but still, Shake Shack is one of my favorite burgers ever.
