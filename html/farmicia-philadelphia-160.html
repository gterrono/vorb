    Listed in Top Restaurants

    I went to Farmicia during Restaurant Week, which is my favorite time of year, and the food was fantastic.  My fiance and I ended up with a table right next to the open window, so we got to enjoy the beautiful early fall weather.  The restaurant was crowded, but we had friendly and speedy service.

    Most important, the food was fantastic.  I love the whole farm-to-table movement, and Farmicia does it well.  Everything was cooked to perfection, and the portions were incredibly large given the restaurant's usual prices.  I'd expected tiny portions for Restaurant Week or a limited menu, but Farmicia let us choose anything from their regular menu for the $35 3-course meal.

    For appetizers, we ordered the crab cake and the crab and Asian pear salad, and both were delicious.  The crab cake was about 2 inches thick and was all fresh crabmeat, with the slightest coating of breading on the outside to give it some crunch.  The crab salad was large and had a generous portion of lump crab meat, as well as lettuce, veggies, avocado puree, and slices of Asian pears.  Yum.

    The entrees were huge, as well.  I had the crispy duck breast, which was cooked to perfection and covered in a cherry reduction.  On the side was a delicious pilaf of wild rice, grain, and pumpkin seeds and a generous helping of perfectly cooked snow peas.  I love seeing green on my plate, and I ate all of the peas, they were so good.  And the duck!  Crispy skin and the meat practically melted in my mouth.  My fiance had the porterhouse, which was nearly as large as the plate.  It was accompanied by some sort of greens and very flavorful mashed potatoes.

    Dessert was also good - decadent flourless chocolate cake with chocolate ice cream and creme brulee.  

    This meal was well worth the $35 and, I think, Restaurant Week is the best time to go to get the greatest amount of food for your money.  The crab cake and crab salad appetizers alone are normally $13.  

    In short: fresh local food, perfectly prepared with friendly and prompt service = five stars.
