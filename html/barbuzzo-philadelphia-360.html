    This place probably deserves a 4 star.  But, I'll stick with the 3 star cause, for me, this place falls in to the category of: you get what you pay for.

    We walked into this place randomly recently while walking around without a reservation.  Surprisingly, for a weekend night, we were seated immediately at the kitchen counter.  There were few empty tables, but I guess they were reserved already.  But, we weren't disappointed at all with the birds eye view of watching the cooks in action.  We wished the cook staff interacted with those sitting at the counter when they weren't making food (just standing there staring at the floor, waiting for next order to come in) - seemed quite arrogant on their part - especially when they could clearly hear us talking about the dishes they were making and the different ingredients they were using.

    The service was prompt.  Could have been more friendly, and less pushy.  The wait staff kept urging us to order two items per person.  Yes, we got it when you mentioned that the serving portion tends to be smaller than other places.  She did the same thing to a couple sitting next to us at the counter, even though they weren't that interested in ordering 4 items between the two of them.

    The food was quite amazing - delicious indeed.  But, I'd say for the level at which this place esteems itself, quite expected.
    Between the three of us, we got: margherita pizza (mehh), mia pera pizza (definitely recommended), casarece pasta (delicious, but not above average), heirloom squash and gorgonzola agnolotti pasta (mouth watering), wild mushroom arancini (interesting), and caciocavallo stuffed meatballs (not too bad indeed).

    So, I'd say this place is worth a try WITH A RESERVATION atleast once.

    I got the farrotto and the meatballs for dinner. The farrotto was delicious with a wonderful texture and flavor. It was not nearly as heavy as risotto, but still carried a lot of its richness and creaminess that is so wonderful. The sharpness of the cheese helped cut through, as well.

    The meatballs were simply the best meatballs I've ever had. The inclusion of spice with the homemade mozzarella was simply wonderful. Again, for the price, I was impressed by the size of the plate.

    I'm eager to try their pizzas in the future. They looked and smelled wonderful.
