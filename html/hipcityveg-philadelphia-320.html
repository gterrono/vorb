    Listed in Vegetarian Wonderland

    THANK YOU Hip City Veg for making food that is not only 100% vegan but also delicious and CHEAP! I only wish that a dozen more of these locations would pop up conveniently located near my apartment, my boyfriend's apartment, my hometown, my job, all of my friends' places, etc...

    As a non-meat eater, it has been driving me crazy being unable to find a quality establishment at which to order quick, cheap, meat-free food that ISN'T a mozzarella-tomato panini or pizza. Don't get me wrong; I love those, but I really do crave quality food that is actually able to offer a wide range of options. Hip City Veg is exactly what I've been looking for and I am so glad I've found it!

    Now for the specifics...

    The Chick'n Ranch sandwich actually angered me at first because it was my first time at Hig City Veg and I was absolutely convinced that I had been given real chicken. It tasted EXACTLY like real fried chicken! It was crazy! Now I didn't become a vegetarian ("pescetarian") to go eating meat-free food that tastes exactly like meat, but this was so delicious I do not care. By far the best meat free chick'n I have ever, ever had.

    Their green lemonade is fantastic and refreshing! Just try it.

    The Philly cheese steak is really great. Its made up mostly of mushrooms, and although I'm not a huge mushroom fan, I still loved it.

    Their veggie burger is really just OK. Tasty, but not the best I've had. I really prefer the veggie burgers that seem to combine lovely things like beans, seeds, and the like into crumbly wonderfulness (Village Whiskey does a great job at this). I think they were going for more of a beef-like veggie burger, but didn't pull it off quite as well as they did for the chick'n. I still have to give this place five stars though because, as you may be able to tell, I'm slightly crazy about it.

    And to top it all off, the girls behind the counter are so friendly! And there is complimentary lemon water there, too. Overall, I say bravo Hip City Veg! You are the plant-based fast food joint of my dreams!
