        This place has more wiz than the Wizard of Oz. Seriously, when you order a cheese steak, they load it up. Pat's, on Passyunk Ave, was spotlighted on the Food Channel's Best of Sandwiches segment. For those of you who don't know, this is their only store, and has been operated by the same family since around 1930.

        Our stop at Pat's was an un-planned visit after a night at Cavanaugh's River Deck on our way home to NJ. After a lifetime of being a Philly steak fanboi, places like Pat's, Geno's and Jim's never gets old. In fact, there are plenty of "go-to" steak joints wherever we are in the City.

        Since I ate throughout the night, I decided to keep this sandwich simple, just a cheese steak with hot peppers on the side. Arlene had the same. The roll was fresher than fresh, and the steak was cooked to perfection. Their was so much cheese on my steak that it drizzled off of the sandwich and onto the plate. While it was a bit of a challenge to eat, I managed to down the whole sandwich in record time.

        Since Pat's is open 24/7, during your next visit to Philadelphia, even if it's in the middle of the night, treat yourself to a steak sandwich.
