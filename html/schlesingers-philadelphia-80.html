    4.5 stars

    Being from NYC, one of the last places I would've chosen to eat at while in Philly would've been a deli. So when our friends suggested we eat at Schlesinger's, my expectations were not very high.

    With that said, we had a great experience there. Pastrami Reuben was excellent, home made french fries were fantastic, matzoh ball soup very good and free pickle/potato salad/pepper bar was a nice bonus. Portions were very good size, prices very reasonable and our server, while a little on the slow side (which was fine being that we weren't in any rush) was very friendly and accomodating.

    I can't believe I am saying this but my next trip from NYC will likely include a stop at Schlesingers (provided I still have time to stop at Jim's for a cheesesteaks).
