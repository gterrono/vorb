    I enjoy this place.  While it is about the same size and footprint as Tria, the vibe is different.  At the end of the day, I prefer Tria, especially the 18th St. Location, just for the windows - which offer natural light and, of course - People Watching. But, Vintage has an awesome wine list, nicely arranged.  My favorite is the "Unusual and Exciting" category, for both reds and whites.  My bartender was very knowledgable, she listened to what I like/dislike - and delivered me a delicious LaMiranda Desecastilla '11 (Somontono, Spain).  I would have never known.  That's the beauty of good wine bars - they educate you. I think the difference between Vintage and Tria is that Vintage is a bit edgier, music with a faster tempo, lighting a tad brighter.  Tria is my Sunday New York Times or date place - just more mellow in a good way.
