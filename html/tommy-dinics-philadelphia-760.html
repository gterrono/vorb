    This sandwich is freaking delicious! You're joking if you didn't like it!

    The roast pork here is tender and juicy, but not so juicy that it soaks through the bun and prevents you from eating it. The quality of roll they put it on is also key. Really great bread for a delicious meat.

    If you can find a seat at the counter, go for it. This place deserves points for being an original and located in the Reading Terminal Market.

    The sandwiches are good, but I would reserve words like "amazing" for some place else. Here are a few points...

    1. Sandwiches are 8.25 or 8.50. Cheese and peppers (sweet or hot) are extra.
    2. The meat is very moist, so much so that the bread (which is soft and fresh) gets soggy fast.
    3. They do not add much cheese when you select that as an option.
    4. The sandwich (I had roast beef) has very little flavour. It needs something else. It is a large sandwich, though. Two people could easily have a good sized snack or small meal off of one.
    5. Service here consists of bringing your food. Don't expect to be offered anything else at any point. Your chair is needed by others.

    Three and a half stars.
