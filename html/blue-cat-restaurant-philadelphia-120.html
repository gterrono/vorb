    I'm giving this place 5 stars because the whole experience was wonderful, and the food was fantastic.  Best meal I had had in a long time.

    It was a rainy evening when two girlfriends and I arrived for a celebratory dinner. Despite the dreary weather, the restaurant was busy and lively. I was really annoyed on how close the tables were since there wasn't much room to move in the small-ish restaurant, but I tried to deal.

    Our table ordered the guacamole, a few of the beef empanadas, oxtail stew, pan-seared salmon, and one of the salads. The menu had a lot of great options, and our server had to help us navigate and select what we should try. The guacamole was pretty irresistible, and the empanadas were a good size as to not ruin our appetite for the other dishes. I chose the salmon, and I was quite surprised. It was a nice delicate flavor of citrus from the mango salad. The other girls also enjoyed their meal.  I wish I could have tasted, but I was too busy finishing my own!

    I'd come here again, and since it's BYO, how could I say no? Prices were reasonable, so your wallet will thank you. I'd absolutely recommend for avocado/guac lovers.

Specialties

Casual indie BYOB specializing in Latin-inspired dishes from Cuba, Puerto Rico, Mexico, Chile and beyond.  Chef purchases daily from local markets, bringing fresh preparation and ingredients to each dish.  Save room for house-made desserts.  Call ahead to request freshly squeezed juice mixes for margaritas, mojitos, daiquiris, sours and more!

History

Established in 2012.

Husband and wife veterans of the food industry bet the farm and opened shop in a sunny new space on the developing fringe of the neighborhood where they live.

Meet the Business Owner

Guy S. Business Owner

Following his training at the CIA, Chef-owner Guy Shapiro began his career in hotels working with classically trained European chefs.  His love of food and travel led him beyond Philadelphia to positions in New Orleans, Montauk, Vail, Memphis, France, Greece and Russia.  His love for Latin flavors has been cultivated by his many Latin co-workers over the years, as well has his own exceptional palette.
