        Listed in In and around Ritten-wait-for-it-HOUSE, my house, Pour me something tall and strong, Gastropubs - The perfect combo of good food and good beer

        Though a very close local establishment to my abode, I have only recently been frequenting this place for business purposes. For the past couple months, I've been coming here for recruiting dinners and sampling a lot of the menu. Though the food and service is OK, there are few places that I go to with extreme frequency and never get tired of the food or atmosphere (Ahem, Sidecar), and this place wouldn't be one of them.

        What it IS good for is a good place to watch sports (first floor), play some tunes on the jukebox while drinking some nice beers, or a casual happy hour spot. There are three levels all with different personalities. As mentioned, first floor is crowded, lots of TVs, music, and a wide tap selection. Second floor is quieter, more suited for a sit down meal, a couple TVs and a smaller bar with less draft selection. Then there is the basement-which is somewhere in between the two. It is dark and loud, perfect for a drunk night out with a group.

        The food is just ok. I've sampled a lot of the menu, as mentioned, and honestly nothing sticks out. They roughly have an English pub theme going on with the menu. The best things in my opinion are the appetizers/bar snacks-really awesome fries. And honestly, what goes better with beer than some good french fries?
