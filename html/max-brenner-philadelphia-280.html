    Cocoa and spice dusted waffle tries.  Like a hundred chocolatey (and non-chocolatey) delicious items on the dessert menu.  Hot chocolate here is actually like liquid chocolate crack.  You come to this place to experience the chocolate, and maybe taste some food in the process.

    It's as close as you can get to Willy Wonka's chocolate factory.  There's a giant chocolate vat at the entrance spinning away.  I wish I could dive into it.

    The atmosphere here is fun, peppy, and happy... basically how chocolate makes you feel.  Diners include kids celebrating birthdays, adults on dates, families and groups enjoying lunch or dinner together, and even business meetings.   There's also a chocolate shop within the restaurants (mm samples!) if you want to pick up something for yourself, or a loved one (hint hint).

    I try to come here a few times a year and save all my calories up that day so I can indulge guilt-free.  The menu is mostly comfort food but there are some salads and healthier options too.  I haven't been yet, but it looks like they also have an awesome brunch menu on the weekend until 4pm, which I plan to hit up soon.

    If you're a chocolate lover, you HAVE to come here.
