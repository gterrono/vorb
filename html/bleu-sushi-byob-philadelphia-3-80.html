    Bleu Sushi is definitely a local secret tucked behind Jefferson Medical School. This small and unassuming spot is known for their good food and good service. My brother and I were greeted by the owner who was very sweet and thanked us for our patronage. He also delivered my brother's dish (Unagi Don) and explained how to eat it since my brother hadn't even ordered it before. This décor is modern and cool blue lighting makes this place a good choice for a BYO date night. Reservations can be made on Opentable, but this spot is off the beaten path so its also good for last minute dining and avoiding crowds. If you're looking for delivery this spot is also on Grub Hub.
    The menu is a bit smaller than the other sushi restaurants  in the area, but our meal was fresh and delicious. The also offer teriyaki and hot sushi entrees. The menu also features some unique dishes you don't see elsewhere such as Kimchee Fries, Korean Chicken Wings, Unagi Don, or Tuna Tataki. Overall I was quite pleased to see that this is a business that deserves all their great Yelp reviews.

Bleu Sushi - Where You Can Escape Your Blues
Welcome to Bleu!
At Bleu Sushi, we believe in creating an intimate atmosphere;
from the size of our restaurant and our service, to our utmost passion; sushi!
We would like to invite you on our journey, where you will experience our unique take in creating succulent Japanese dishes that are paired with an unparalleled eye to detail, without sacrificing an ounce of quality.
Keeping in symmetry with our Executive Chef's and owner's mantra of
"High Quality - No Rip Offs",
we trust that our dishes speak for themselves. We do not believe in overcharging for high quality dishes, as we rather you break out a dance after eating at Bleu, than breaking your bank.
We are open for lunch and dinner, as well as the option for delivery and take out.
We like to think that our lunch specials are an accurate depiction of the word "special"; where we do not forego on the array of delicious accompaniments to your main dish.
Come in for a romantic dinner or a big celebration, as we would be honored to host your special occasions.
We Look Forward To Serving You Here At Bleu!
