    Possibly the king of the carts in Philly. Real, home-cooked food is what you get here. Christos, the falafel nazi, or whatever you want to call him is a master of his trade.     This food truck is a must for any Philadelphian. I've been here a half-dozen times because its right down the street from my office, and I have never been disappointed. Because as the Greek man says, "It's all about quality."

    The truck is easy to find, just follow your nose to the delicious smells wafting at the corner of 20th and Market St., or look for the long line of 10-20 customers waiting patiently for their food. Don't be deterred by the line, the prize at the end is worth the wait.

    This guy runs a tight ship. There is only one menu item, and it will consist of a delicious protein (usually chicken), delicious falafels, delicious veggies (fresh), and whatever other goodness he has that day. Sometimes he'll thrown in a fresh apple. Sometimes he'll throw in the most delicious berries you've never heard of. Whatever it is, it'll be good, and your taste buds will thank you. The cost is about $8-9 per meal, and a $1 tip is customary. It's all worth it in the end, however, because the food he cooks is the best street meat you'll ever find.

Specialties

Welcome to Falafel Truck! Every day is a different experience of Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisine. Everything is prepared fresh and cooked on my charcoal grill.

History

Established in 1990.
