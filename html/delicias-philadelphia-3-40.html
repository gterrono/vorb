        All hail Delicias!

        I came here at South Street Night Market and waited 45 minutes for my Pabellon arepa. It was well worth it! The corn shell was stuffed with beef, black beans, and plantains.       Specialties

      Delicias serves up fresh, authentic arepas (grilled corn patties, gluten and wheat free) and latin food from our two trucks on 40th & Locust and 35th & Market Streets in University City, and at events around town. Our hand-made arepas come hot off the griddle, stuffed with your choice of housemade fillings, from fried sweet plantains to slow-cooked shredded beef, chicken or grilled spicy pork sausage (chorizo), pico de gallo, guacamole and cotija cheese...simple deliciousness on wheels.

      History

      Established in 2011.

      Delicias first opened its service window in 2011.  It was awarded first place in Manayunk Street Festival for a roast pork and apple pico de gallo arepa, and received the 2012 Penndy Award.  Delicias has been featured on Fox News, Philadelphia City Paper, Philadelphia Inquirer, and as one of Philly's "To Do's."

      Meet the Business Owner

      Lynette G. Business Owner

      Lynette Gueits Sutton is the owner of the Delicias' trucks on UPenn's campus . She is a graduate of Johns Hopkins University and Wharton School of Business. Lynette is delighted to share her passion for cooking and love of classic Latin street food with the Philadelphia community.
