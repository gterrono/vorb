    Corned Beef Heaven. That's the only way to describe it. I absolutely LOVE their hot corned beef sandwiches. The owner of Hershel's has never heard the term "less is more". The sandwiches are piled HIGH with meat. So much so that I usually take half the meat off and eat it by itself before I even dig into the sandwich. They carve the meats right in front of you as you order your sandwich. They serve the corned beef on real Jewish rye bread with a nice spicy mustard or a great tasting thousand island dressing and a wedge of Jewish pickle, the way it's supposed to be! They have hot corned beef, hot turkey, hot pastrami, chips, soups, sides and sodas. The place is always busy....and it's warranted. I've been there about 10 times and out of those ten times, I had the perfect sandwich 9 times. The 10th was good, but a little on the dry side, that's the only reason I didn't give them 5 stars!

    The pastrami and corned beef is made on site, which makes all the difference. Now yes I've been to 4th street, done the Carnegie Deli, and had many well known pastrami sandwiches, but the pastrami here really takes the cake.

    So let's boil it down: try the sandwich, don't have a heart attack, and PM me to tell me all about how you fell in love.
