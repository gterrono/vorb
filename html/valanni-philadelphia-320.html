    Wow! Easily one of the best meals I've had in a while. And for the price you'll pay, it's a steal! Stop reading and make your reservation now.

    If you don't follow instructions (like me) then you're still reading, so I'll keep it short. Our table of four ordered 3 tapas, 1 entree, 1 paella (for one), and 2 desserts. My absolute can't miss is the duck entree. It was presented beautifully and cooked to perfection. It made me genuinely "mmmmm"!!

    But everyone at our table really loved everything else we ordered- lobster mac & cheese with gigantic chunks of lobster (not kidding! See picture!) was pretty stellar as well. Paella is not my favorite dish but our seafood paella was pretty great. Oreo beignet is a must try. Really- the other desserts are tempting but go for the beignet!

    Service was impeccable. Our waitress Rachelle staged all of our orders into courses. It was a nice touch coming from a tapas restaurant, where it's easy to feel overwhelmed with all the things coming and going from the table. The service really made the meal.

    I liked the atmosphere of the restaurant. It was just young and trendy enough. They still maintained a classic feel with the wood-top tables and clearing of plates at each course.

    Bonus! Free sangria when you check in on yelp!

    Total cost before tip was $167 and that's with two rounds of drinks (before yelp discount) for FOUR people. Sometimes you pay that much for two people and still don't have the experience I did.

    If I lived in Philadelphia, this would be my go-to for all special occasions, birthdays, etc. The hardest part is choosing what to order because this chef and their staff simply don't miss on execution!

    NOW go and make your reservation!!!

History

Established in 2000.

Valanni restaurant opened its doors in September of 2000, introducing "Medi-Latin" cuisine to Philadelphia. Situated right off of the "Avenue of the Arts," Valanni has become a pre-theater ritual and place to meet for a night-cap for many who live in and around the neighborhood. The bar at Valanni keeps up with the creative cuisine by offering an assortment of sangria, mojitos and other specialty drinks. The kitchen is open until 1 AM daily, serving Tapas and a Mediterranean inspired lounge menu.
