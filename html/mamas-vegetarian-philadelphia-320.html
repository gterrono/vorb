    Similar to other reviewers, I had avoided this place for years simply because "Vegetarian" was in the name.  Curiosity finally got the best of me about a month ago, and I'm so glad it did.  I'm am now filled with falafel on weekly basis.

    Rundown:
    Get the Mama's sandwich
    Whole wheat or regular pita
    Get it hot or not
    A scoop of creamy hummus layers the pita
    3 heavenly falafel balls line the bottom
    Tomatoes, cucumbers, pickled cabbage next
    2 more of those delicious falafel balls on top
    I get extra hummus on top instead of the tahini

    Outcome:
    Delicious, crispy falafel balls that are soft on the inside and not dry.  This is mixed with the perfect crunch from the fresh cucumbers and the pickled cabbage, which also provides a nice vinegar taste.  The contrast of textures and flavors is simply amazing.

    Just raised the price to $7, but completely worth it.  Cash only.

    You can also sometimes snag a free piece of fruit from the counter. Boom!
