    We have always wanted to try out the famous Sabrina's Cafe, and were privileged to spend some time with friends at this famous spot in Fairmount.

    Let me start by saying this place was packed!  People waiting outside, on the staircase and on the sidewalk waiting patiently for the waiter to call their names.  The smell was incredible, smokey breakfast goodness.  We were seated in 15 mins and ordered some liquid energy (coffee) to start.

    I will be back, and probably try the other locations as well!

Specialties

Based off the same love for food, quality ingredients, and good friends that flourished Sabrina's Cafe in the Italian Market; Sabrina's Cafe and Spencer's Too hopes to satisfy even the loneliest of tummy's with behemoth portions and decadent fare no matter what time of the day it is. You can take that to the bank.

Checkout our Breakfast, Brunch, and Dinner. And did you know that we Cater to?

History

Established in 2007.

Sabrina's Cafe was born out of Robert & Raquel DeAbreu's desire to create a comfortable, welcoming place that served comfortable, welcoming food but with a twist. In May 2001, the first Sabrina's Café opened and was appropriately named after the couples new baby, Sabrina Isabella. With the help of impassioned regulars, Sabrina's began to outgrow it's modest upbringings and a second location was added in 2007 named Sabrina's Cafe & Spencer's Too, after their son.
