    Went to Maggiano's for dinner with my wife and it was amazing. Being an Indian, I absolutely love Indian food but when I want to have something different, I go out for Italian food as it is my second favorite cuisine. So I have been to a lot Italian restaurants and Maggiano's is one of my favorites on the list.

    There was a little bit of wait to get the table which is understandable because we went during the dinner rush. We were greeted properly by the hostess and got a table in less 10 minutes.

    Food:-
    We ordered calamari, Chicken Marsala,Chicken Francese and couple of drinks. The flavors were spot on. Chicken was tender and juicy. The flavors were amazing. This is my go to meal at any Italian place and I have to say it was one of the best I have ever had when compared to other places that I have been to.

    Service:-
    The waiter was very polite and professional. Took our order in a timely manner. We got our drinks and food in time and didn't have to wait for a long time considering the fact that it was a busy night. Checked on us periodically to see if we needed something else. We even joked about how I was served my drink in a girly glass but who cares. It was amazing and I am not ashamed to admit it.

    I will definitely come back and eat here again gladly. I highly recommend this place to anyone who is living in Philly or just visiting.

    When I entered the restaurant, I was in an absolutely miserable mood having just received some the worst news of my life. Thus I did not expect to enjoy my supper. However, the excellence of the meal totally changed my attitude!

    I order the steamed mussels to precede the meal and lobster carbonara. The food was very tasty! The mussels came with a couple of pieces of garlic toast that I used to sop up the broth... to die for!

    The when the lobster carbonara came, it was loaded with lobster meat. The sauce was a little thinner that I was expected but I think that is due to the chef making the meal healthier. The taste was superb and reflected a high quality restaurant.

    Because the meal was so good, I decided to order dessert. I had the Zuccocca (sp?) cake. For all you chocolate lovers out there, don't pass this one up. I also order a double espresso and a dark sambuca on the side so that I could mix the sambuca and espresso according to how I liked it. (Often times, in other restaurants, if it premixed, they either put too much in or not enough.) Everything was great and I still have enough sambuca left over for yet another espresso!

    All in all, this was an outstanding experience and a I left the restaurant in a very good mood as well as being sated.
