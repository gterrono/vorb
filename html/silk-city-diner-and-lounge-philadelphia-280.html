        This is my go-to diner for dinner out with the girls, or anytime I'm in the mood for something a little different. Silk City is obviously best when the beer garden is open in the summer, but I still find myself here plenty in the colder months. I like that the menu changes seasonally, the food is consistently good, and the servers are friendly and accommodating. My faves here are the baked mac and cheese appetizer and the chocolate banana bread pudding dessert with ice cream. Now here's a tragic story about that very dessert:

        I went to Silk City on a Saturday night for a double date. After a nice dinner, even though we were full, I convinced my date to get the chocolate banana bread pudding and take it home, since I had been talking about it non-stop for weeks. We did just that, and ended up putting it in his freezer to enjoy at a later date. Fast forward 3 weeks: I remember that the dessert is still sitting in the freezer, waiting to be eaten. This makes me very happy, so I go to the freezer, open the door...and can't find the bread pudding. It was not buried in the depths of the freezer. It was straight up gone. This mystery remains unsolved, and I am deeply disturbed by it. I had been looking forward to eating this bread pudding for upwards of a month at this point. I will seriously think twice about leaving delicious desserts in unattended freezers again.
