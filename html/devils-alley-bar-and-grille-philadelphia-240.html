    We went for a Saturday morning brunch.  The restaurant was busy, but not packed.  Our table was cramped in a corner by the stairs and I wasn't a huge fan of the table/chair; they were uncomfortable for my size.  We sat upstairs and unfortunately had the bartender as our waiter.  After we got the food, he didn't come back to check if we needed anything or to drop the check for about 20 minutes.  We didn't really mind since we were taking our time before walking around town, but for somebody in a rush it would have been an annoyance.

    We had the mixed griddle (corn pancake, french toast, eggs, bacon) and the bacon/scallion omelet.  Both meals were good, the best part being the potatoes and the corn pancake.

    They have revolving brunch specials, and I was very tempted to try the Oreo Bread Pudding French Toast.  

    Overall, the food was good but service was a bit lacking and our table was an afterthought.
