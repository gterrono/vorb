    Listed in BYOB is A-OK

    Two things to know first....1) it's cash only....2) it's byob.

    On a lovely Saturday we decided to stop and sit outside on their sidewalk seating...which was a great call. We got to enjoy the beautiful day and the service was absolutely great! Me and my friend are both gluten free and dairy free so we asked for veggies substituted for cheese on the salad and they were more than accommodating.

    We got their seafood dish including flounder and calamari in a lemon olive oil sauce and it was amazing! So tasty and light! We also got a salad and mussels and the salad was a great portion size for sharing. The mussels were ok, not the best I have had but still good.

    We only walked inside for a couple seconds to get seating but it looked very inviting and intimate inside as well.

    9/27/2014

    Came here on a Thursday night around 9pm with a bottle of red. The atmosphere was dimly lit and sexy.  Besides an obnoxious drunken birthday party of 4 females, we were the only couple in the restaurant.  

    Food: We got the lobster/seafood stuffed ravioli, salmon special, risotto, and gnocchi. The food was delicious...but a little luke warm. The portions were reasonably sized.

    Service: The waitress was super friendly, helpful and had a delightful demeanor.

    Overall, food was delicious, ambiance was perfect (eclectic decorations), but service was a little questionable.  The owner/manager hussled us out of the restaurant immediately after the birthday party had left...They started mopping the floors as we were finishing up our entree and still  sipping on wine. The scent of pine-sol mixed with bleach definitely didn't add to to the aroma of the salmon special.
