    Pietro's offers casual and affordable dining right off Rittenhouse Square. Unlike most Italian restaurants nearby, here they have a full service bar, delivery, online ordering and low prices with family-sized portions. Service is always consistent. Personally I've stopped in for lunch or dinner many times, but I don't know if I would go here for a first date because it has a much more casual feel and the interior looks a little cheesy (like a chain Italian restaurant). But it is nice that they have some outdoor seating (about 6 tables for parties of two usually).

    The menu here also has a lot of options between pizza, pasta, and entrée dishes. Pizza's come individually sized or larger, the pasta and entrée dishes are also offered in family-sized portions or individually. The nice thing is that even the individual portions are very large (I have taken home leftovers from every time I have dined here). The menu is large so if you need some suggestions here you go...
    For appetizers try the Stuffed Mushrooms (fresh mushrooms, stuffed with four cheeses and spinach), Fried Mozzarella, Grilled Shrimp (large shrimp, wrapped with fresh zucchini and stuffed with fresh mozzarella, served over spicy marinara sauce), or the Risotto Balls Suppli (fried rice balls stuffed with mozzarella cheese and seasoned ground beef, served with marinara sauce).
    For salads I love the Insalata Mozzarella Fresca, Insalata Florentine, or Insalata la Grecca (no lettuce in this salad).

    You cannot leave without trying a pizza! "Designed and built by hand the coal oven at Pietro's delivers a unique flavor and bake that is not possible from wood or
    gas ovens. The oven reaches heat of up to 1200 degrees. The intense heat of oven and coal evenly bakes the pies to create Pietro's crispy and smokey crust". My favorite pizza that you MUST try is the Barese (spicy roasted sausage, hot pesto, tomato, basil and mozzarella) or Create Your Own! *Whole Wheat crust is available.

    For your main course I would also suggest trying AT LEAST one pasta dish :) everyone I know thoroughly enjoys the Rigatoni All Vodka (rigatoni pasta in a tomato cream sauce with red onions, pork pancetta, vodka and pecorino cheese), Gemelli, Arugula é Pollo (twisted pasta, with chicken breast, garlic, arugula, grape
    tomatoes and pecorino cheese, in a white wine sauce), Linguini Cielo é Mare (chicken breast and shrimp, sautéed with onions, garlic, white wine and pecorino cheese, served in a creamy tomato sauce), or Fettuccine Gardinera (fettuccine tossed with seasonal vegetables, garlic and olive oil, in a white wine, chicken broth percorino cheese sauce).
    Otherwise the Pollo Villaggio, Vittello con Fungh, and Melanzane Parmigiana are also stellar.

    It's Italian so if you're coming here be ready to eat! Mangia!
