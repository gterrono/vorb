    The first time I stepped into Tampopo, I thought "Cool, this place has rice bowls." It has since become a staple in my diet, which is impressive since it's a bit of a hike from my office and requires a drive and park in Center City if I'm heading over from my apartment. Neither of these factors have deterred me from getting Tampopo for lunch and the occasional dinner. It's cheap, it's tasty, and everything is cooked on the spot-- we all know that it's all too easy for an Asian place to just scoop meat from pre-prepared vats. The rice bowls are definitely the strong suit, but the full box meal is nice as well. Sushi has some room for improvement; the selection is somewhat limited and the taste and preparation could be better, however a single sushi roll is on the largish side. As an added bonus, the food isn't greasy at all so you can almost fool yourself into thinking you're eating healthy! What more can I say? If you haven't tried it already, stop on by and roll the dice, it'll only be like $7.

    Important notes: If you're part of the work lunch crowd you can call ahead or get there around noon. If you walk in around 12:15 you could be waiting in cramped quarters for a while. Also, credit card purchases must be for more than $10 or $15. I forget which.
