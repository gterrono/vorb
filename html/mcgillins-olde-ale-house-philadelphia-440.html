Specialties

Philadelphia's Oldest Pub, featuring 29 beers on draft focusing on local, fresh micro brews.  A pub menu loaded with homemade items, everything from our famous Nachos and French Onion Soup to the Irish Mixed Grill and Cape May Stew.

McGillin's is considered the BEST place for karaoke in the city - Sundays and Wednesdays are hopping with DJ Joe at the mic.

History

Established in 1860.

Philly's Oldest Pub!

Meet the Business Owner

Chris M. Business Owner

Christopher Mullins, Jr. runs McGillin's Olde Ale House with his parents, Mary Ellen & Chris Mullins, Sr. They purchased it from Mary Ellen's father & uncle (Christopher's grandfather & great-uncle). They're only the second family to run the historic tavern in its 150+ year history. McGillin's is the oldest continuously operating tavern in Philadelphia and one of the oldest in the country. Christopher believes that beer is the eternal equalizer - friends have been made, marriages begun, wars ended. If we can ignite camaraderie and friendship than we have done our job!
