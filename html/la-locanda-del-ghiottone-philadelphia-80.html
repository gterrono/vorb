    As much as  La loconda is my fav Italian restaurant in Philly and therefore tell everyone to go try it out, this time I wanted to give it 3.5~4 instead of 5.

    Plus, it's BYOB! How wrong can you go w/ this!

    However, the reason I deduct one star time is because the younger server (there's always the same 2 guys- the uncle and the cousin) is so crazy and unorganized and not careful. Thank god the food here is so delish it overcomes the service of this one rude server!...I complain about him all the time,,,but I will come back for the food. It's that good hehe

    This is definitely one of my fave Italian restaurants in Philly! I've been coming to La Locanda Del Ghiottone for awhile now and it has never been a disappointment.

See, their food will leave you craving more (like I could seriously go for some mushroom crepes right now). Everything is just so NOM.
