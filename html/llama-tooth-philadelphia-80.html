    There are few places I feel truly deserve a 5 star review - and Llama Tooth is one of them for a variety of reasons.  Firstly, the outdoor space is incredible! It's really well set up, well decorated, and the mural and landscaping are pretty much intertwined in a remarkable way.  The space is just great, especially in the early fall, summer, and spring.  In fact, if it wasn't that cold in the winter, it might be cool to hang out in the patio then too.
