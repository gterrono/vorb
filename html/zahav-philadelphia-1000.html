    Stopped by on a Saturday night around 8 without a reservation. The place was full and reservations were needed for tables, but the bar spots were first come first serve. Put our name in and waited our turn. The staff is quite friendly and welcoming. There is a very relaxed atmosphere. We were offered drinks in the small waiting area which was great. Were able to get a spot after about 45min or so. The cocktails were very well made. The wine list is unique and (from what I tried) very good. The staff is knowledgeable about the food and the drinks. Turkish Hummus was great as was the salad (a variety of 6 small veggie plates). Ordered a few other small plates that didn't disappoint. The dessert was also incredible. The whole experience was great and we left very satisfied. Food is excellent. Need a reservation for a table on Saturday but can probably snag a bar spot if you are willing to wait. Drinks offered while you wait so it's not bad at all.
