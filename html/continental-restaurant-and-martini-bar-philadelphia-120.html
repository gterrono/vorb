    TOO MUCH SALT...

    Contintental is typical Steven Starr, fun concepts, delicious overpriced drinks, and decent food.  The martini bar theme is fun for a night out with friends outside of my usual dive bar and taqueria hangouts.

    On a recent trip here I had a pretty delicious dirty martini and my dining partner had something fruity and garnished with licorice which was admittedly pretty adorable. I have no complaints about the drinks other than the slightly too high price point.

    The food on the other hand was really hit or miss. The fries, and lobster mac and cheese were good tasting and filling junk food. The mexican pizza with shrimp and french onion soup dumplings were the saltiest foods I have eaten in some time. The french onion dumplings sounded promising but tasted asif I were eating a boullion cube dumpling, do not order these unless you really really enjoy salt.

    The wait staff was friendly and not at all overbearing which I appreciate. I split a "tapas" style dessert which is perfect to share with one person. One of the main reasons I never order dessert is because they are way too large, here the smaller portions are a nice ending.

    Basically come for drinks and bar snacks, but be careful what you order.

Specialties

This stainless steel 60's-style diner offers an exciting menu of global cuisine, including crab pad thai, cheesesteak eggrolls and lobster mashed potatoes. Famed for its sensational cocktails, the extensive bar features a wide variety of libations and over 15 types of martinis.

Meet the Business Owner

Stephen S. Business Owner

Founded in 1995 by Stephen Starr, Philadelphia-based STARR Restaurants is one of the fastest growing multi-concept restaurant companies in the country. STARR Restaurants include Buddakan, Morimoto, The Continental, Barclay Prime, Continental Mid-Town, El Vez, Jones, Alma de Cuba, Pod, Parc, Butcher and Singer, Pizzeria Stella, El Rey, Ranstead Room, The Dandelion, Talula's Garden, Frankford Hall, Il Pittore, Route 6 and seasonal food-stand SquareBurger in Philadelphia, as well as, Morimoto and Buddakan in New York City's thriving Meatpacking District. STARR Restaurants expanded to Atlantic City, NJ, with Buddakan and The Continental located in the Pier Shops at Caesars in 2006, and to Florida with Steak 954 at the W Hotel Fort Lauderdale in 2009, and Makoto in the Bal Harbour Shops in 2011. For more information, please visit www.starr-restaurant.com.
