    I was looking to introduce my boyfriend to the Mexican torta and to our luck the Mexican taco truck at the local park didn't show up. But we found this place! Reasonably priced, very mom-and-pop and cozy, which is exactly what I like about a place because it's usually where you find the best food. Don't let the neighborhood fool you into thinking this place is not worth it.

    We had the huevo con chorizo torta and torta al pastor. Big serving, enough to have the other half for dinner. The Mexican hot chocolate was delicious. My boyfriend is an iced coffee addict and he really enjoyed the iced coffee.  For dessert we had churros. Churros are one of my favorite desserts so I know a thing or two about churros. The churros were crunchy and doughy, but lacked a bit of flavor.  The dipping chocolate was more liquidity than I was used to. There was a good selection of desserts, so maybe go for the flan or tres leches (another favorite dessert of mine).

    The staff was really friendly. We didn't have to wait long for our food. Overall, we had a great experience and I would highly recommend this place for a low-key, tasty meal.

We intend to come again!

Specialties

For an interesting global twist on traditional Mexican cuisine, look no further than Cafe Con Chocolate! Located in the heart of Philadelphia, our cozy little cafe provides a uniquely bohemian atmosphere for you to relax and enjoy life's delicacies. Join us for good chocolate, tasty Mexican fare and samplings from around the world.

We use only the finest ingredients available, and each of our dishes are made on-site daily. From familiar favorites such as nachos, tacos, burritos, empanadas and tamales to homemade salsas, Molletes, Chilaquiles and Motuleños Especiales, your craving for Mexican food is sure to be satisfied at Cafe Con Chocolate. Don't forget to pair your meal with one of our signature hot chocolate drinks! If you have specific dietary needs, don't worry - we have plenty of healthy options available, and can accommodate both vegetarian and vegan diners. Cafe Con Chocolate es una tradición que no se olvida!

History

Established in 2007.

Much like our food, Cafe Con Chocolate truly started from scratch. From making openings in the walls for windows to our brightly painted exterior, we wanted our passion for Mexican cooking to show in every detail of our business. Our friendly and professional staff will go out of their way to make sure your dining experience is as enjoyable as possible, so make sure to stop by soon!

Meet the Business Owner

Arturo L. Business Owner

Arturo and the rest of the Cafe Con Chocolate staff are proud of the great reputation we have earned over the past several years, and look forward to serving the Philadelphia community for many more to come. We love bringing traditional Mexican flavors to the area, and are committed to serving fresh, tasty food to our customers every day. Whether you're familiar with authentic Mexican cuisine or you've never tried it, we look forward to serving you!
