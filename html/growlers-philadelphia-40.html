    Listed in Italian Market/ South Philly

    I've had dinner here twice and really enjoyed myself. I didn't spend much time in the bar room, but I absolutely love the look and feel of the dining room. Low lighting with a huge fireplace surrounded by big comfy couches. Unfortunately those seats were taken, but next time, they're mine!!!

    One of my favorite things about this place was the little dart room by the bathrooms. I LOVE playing darts and can't wait to play during a happy hour sometime.

    Also, the service is fast and friendly. Our server, Natalie, took GREAT care of us and even offered for us to try samples from their impressive draft list before ordering. As eclectic beer drinkers, this is greatly appreciated!

    The first visit, 3 of us shared a Growler of this crazy, 9.5% Palate Wrecker IPA they had on draft. We each had a little over 2 glasses. After one glass we were all feeling a buzz, so beware with this one! Delicious but very intense! A little more citrus than I expected with such a strong beer, but as a fan of wheat beer, I loved it. The next time, we went with our own pints and loved the variety of their drink offerings.

    The food was great both times we went. We've tried a little bit of everything. Appetizers-- smoked salmon (favorite! comes with a fried egg and an AMAZING sweet potato latke...wish it came with 5!), quinoa salad, Mussels, Mac and Cheese with Pulled Pork, Growlers Wings (favorite!) and Truffled Fries with pecorino cheese. I'd order any of these again, they were all delicious.

    For entrees, I've had the BBQ Lamb with a potato pie and loved it. I was having a serious lamb craving and this did not disappoint! The meat is smoked and shredded, topped with a sweet and savory BBQ sauce. My boyfriend had the Pork Belly BLT and seriously could not stop talking about how it was his favorite sandwich he'd ever had. Served on a Sarcone's roll (the only roll a sandwich should be served on...), it had maple glazed pork combined with the BLT. Even though he was full, I could see what restraint it took for him to take half of it home for lunch the next day...he almost didn't make it!

    The one thing that bummed us all out was the fact they have NO DESSERT MENU :-( We were all aching for some sweets after our meal, but couldn't indulge. I hope they get some dessert soon!!

    This place can get a little pricey if you're boozing. The food is semi-expensive- appetizers are small portions, entrees were a great size, but it is very high-quality. I say WORTH IT!  We met the owner during our meal and he was a great guy. I love meeting good people who want to genuinely deliver with great food. This guy has got a great thing going at Growlers.

Specialties

Growlers is a bar, kitchen and living room in the Bella Vista section of Philadelphia.

We're a beer-centric neighborhood pub offering great food and a relaxing environment, open Monday thru Saturday from 5pm to 2am, Sundays from 11am to 2am. Our kitchen is open daily until 1am.

We have a rotating draft list of craft beer that is available by the pint or in growlers. We also offer beer in bottles and cans, and have a serious selection of spirits. Build your own 6 packs and growlers togo are available until 2am daily.

Our living room features a wood burning fireplace, and we have a game room downstairs for darts, as well as a large selection of board games to keep you occupied. Wednesday nights feature Free For All Comedy sessions, an open mic night showcasing local talent.

Come join us!

History

Established in 2012.

Growlers was born from a collaboration between two already well-established restaurateurs in the city. Partners Jason Evenchik (Vintage, Time, Garage, & Bar) and Jay Willard, a man with a long history of experience at Starr Restaurant establishments in his back pocket (including The Dandelion, El Vez, Jones, Frankford Hall, and Parc), took over the former Vesuvio to launch their newest venture.
