        Every time I go to Philly I try a new cheesesteak place.  There are good spots, great spots, and awesome spots.  This is one of those places that falls under the "awesome spots" category.

        I love a great, local, hole-in-the-wall joint and that is what this place is.  Cosmi's is a little neighborhood deli located in south Philly that I'd have just driven right by if I hadn't found it on yelp.  It's old and it looks like it hasn't been updated since they opened the place.  You won't see Cosmi's in some local advertisement.  You won't see it featured on any tv food shows.  It's just quietly serving up one of the best cheesesteaks I've ever tasted.

        First of all, even though they have won local awards for their cheesesteaks they are still flying under the radar.  There is no crowd waiting to get in.  You can walk right in, order your food, and you'll have it in just a few minutes.  Cash or credit.  You can eat in if you'd like at one of their two "fancy" card tables with folding chairs or you can take it to go.   I got my cheesesteak with sharp provolone on a fresh seeded roll.  You can get cheese whiz if you'd like too and get regular bread but their crispy seeded rolls are amazing!    

        If you are passing through town and are looking for a great cheesesteak bypass the usual touristy places like Pat's or Geno's and stop by Cosmi's and get the real deal!  It's as authentic as they come and it's not far off I-95.  Enjoy!
