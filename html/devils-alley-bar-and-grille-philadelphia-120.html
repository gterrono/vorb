        We celebrated Beer Week this year at Devil's Alley (19th and Chestnut) which is offering a value priced $30 pp 3 course dinner with beer pairings.

        The food was very good, with the highlights being the desserts- bread pudding paired with a glass of framboise, and chocolate brownie sundae accompanied by a chocolate stout were both delicious!

        Main courses of batter fried catfish with sweet potato fries, and a tender sliced hanger steak with spinach were also very tasty, albeit paired with somewhat forgettable beers..

        Great value for the price, my only knock on the place is the loud music and lackluster service.
