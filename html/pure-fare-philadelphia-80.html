    Just moved from LA in February, and was hoping to find a "west coast" style healthy spot for salads and light, nutritious meals. Lo and behold, two blocks away - I found it.

    Pure Fare works a bit like an upscale Sainsbury's in England. Cold sandwiches and salads are pre made and set in fridges along the wall. You can walk right in and grab one if you're in a rush. To the left is the sandwich/quiche counter (with frittatas and other baked items for breakfast). To the right a juice and coffee bar.

    They serve blue bottle here (a San Francisco roaster), and both the drip and espresso are quite good.

    If you want a sandwich and a coffee or juice, paying gets a little weird with two counters, but it's no biggie.

    The sandwiches I've had so far are great. Dense, grainy breads and healthy, mostly vegetarian / vegan options. The salmon and salad combo with yogurt sauce is crazy good. Also quite enjoyed the artichoke quiche.

    All these healthy, tasty options and the price is not bad at all, considering. Something like this in LA would likely run you $10 to $15 and here the sandwiches don't seem to exceed $8, and are filling.

    To seal the "LA vibe", there are little water bowls for small pets by the window and of course, women in yoga pants. Like a little slice of Santa Monica.

    I'd recommend it to anyone who wants to stop by and have a quick bite.
