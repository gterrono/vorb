    Wow; I just browsed through some other reviews for Santucci's, and there are certainly people at opposite ends of the spectrum!  I have had take out pizza from Santucci's in Northeast Philly a few times over the years; though it was usually for a work function so I never had to actually place the order myself.  

    This past weekend I ordered two large pies to take to the Phillies tailgate.  I originally planned on getting a tomato pie from Sarcone's but I waited until last minute.  I called the night before I needed the pizzas to make sure I could pick them up by 11 AM.  The kid seemed kind of confused and told me that he put the order in but that I should call back to confirm my order in the morning.  When I called back, same kid answered and he had my order.  Everything was fresh, hot and as I ordered when I picked it up at 11 AM.  I was pleased that they offered a honey-wheat crust and everyone devoured the pies at the tailgate.  

    No complaints here, except they are a tad over priced at $16 each for a plain pizza and a tomato pie... good thing I wasn't getting any toppings!
