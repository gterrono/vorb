    Pretty good Cuban-inspired menu. Nice ambience, friendly attentive staff. Only wished that they had Picadillo on the menu. But the Tres Leches cake dessert makes up for it.

    My favorites: Plato Vegetariano (rice, vegetables, arepa, yucca, avocado, sweet and green plantains) and the Arroz con Pollo Cubano (chicken, cuban sausage, saffron rice, maduros, and manzanilla olives). There are two versions of the classic Paella dish - the seafood heavy "Valenciana" with shrimp, clams, octopus, lobster, squid, mussels and scallops, chicken, Cuban chorizo, rice and plantains in a saffron broth, and the "Vegetariana" with chickpeas, green peas, carrots, zucchini, shitake mushrooms, plum tomatoes, broccoli, avocado and asparagus in saffron broth.

    Decent drinks, an offering of smoothies and cafe con leche, cortadito and cafecito to make you think of Calle Ocho in Miami.

    I love Mixto although as a vegetarian I only get the vegetarian platter if I'm there for dinner.  It doesn't necessarily sound that exciting (just veggies and rice and beans) but it's a huge plate of food that's always seasoned just right.  And you can't beat the price.  It's one of my favorite vegetarian meals in the city (that's not counting places that really cater to vegetarians/ vegans).  

    I specifically wanted to write a review about their brunch.  Went there the other day and LOVED it.  I always love when I can find tofu scramble for brunch.  Pancakes are great but sometimes I just want protein and a more savory brunch.  Mixto has pancakes and french toast (which looked really good but we didn't get), but I got the Vegan Gallo Pinto, which had tofu scramble, rice & beans, plantains, cassava, and vegan sausage.  I will say the tofu wasn't the best... it was okay, but could be seasoned better, but as a whole the meal was really good.  Great flavors, filling, and a little different than other tofu scrambles.  My husband had the regular Gallo Pinto which he also loved.  Our whole brunch (with an appetizer) was just $31.  It was a really great deal and great food.  I definitely recommend.
