    Black & Brew is a nice little breakfast/brunch spot that can easily be overlooked. Its right on the corner of 15th & East Passyunk Ave. Just walking by this place it appears small and divvy, it's very easy to overlook. Once you walk in,  order off of their menu, and sit down with your La Columbe coffee your thoughts will then change. A lot of locals frequent this place for breakfast and many come in for their morning cup of coffee.

    They have quite an extensive menu and are very accommodating. They have dairy alternatives like Soy Milk and have egg whites vs. real eggs. I ordered the omelets with spinach, tomato, goat cheese and asked for egg whites. That came with hash browns and bread. The omelets was really good. The goat cheese seemed to melt into the omelets really enhancing its flavor. The taste of the hash browns were very home like. They use actual real potatoes and season them well.

    I also ordered the chai tea latte with soy. It was very smooth and definitely good to the last drop. Everyone here is friendly and attentive. The prices aren't bad for the quality of food that you get. The only downfall here, is that Black and Brew, can get super busy and its really on the small side. So sometimes there isn't really an open table. You can however always take your food to go.

    Black and Brew is a cute little coffee/sandwich shop on E. Passyunk on the edge of Passyunk Square.  This shop opens at 7 am, so you can get your coffee fix in the area while running your errands or heading to/from the gym or work.  There are a lot of vegetarian and some vegan selections at this spot (I would say about 50% of the menu), as well as a hearty selection of sandwiches and salad for meat eaters.  

    I came here on my way to play football on a beautiful Saturday morning.  The inside of the restaurant was bustling and the outside sidewalk tables were filled to capacity.  I ordered a coffee and egg, cheese, and veggie sausage bagel sandwich to go.  The coffee was quick and delicious.  I would describe the coffee as a rich medium roast. They also have an espresso machine and can make you any variety of specialty coffee drinks.  The bagel was decent (after four years of living in NYC, I have some bagel snobbery issues)--they have several options--I went with everything.  The veggie sausage was so good (and convincing) that I checked several times to make sure I wasn't eating pork.  The egg was scrambled (I wish there was an option to get it over-easy--though I probably could have if I asked) and the cheese tasted like cheddar.  Overall the sandwich was huge, relatively cheap for a non-fast food restaurant (it was ~$6 total for the coffee and sandwich), and good.  My wait time from ordering to walking out the door was probably around 7 minutes--not bad at all for a busy Saturday morning.

    I will definitely be back again to sample what looked to be a delicious lunch menu.  They offer "daily" specials on their web site, as well, so you can check out the changing menu before you go.

Specialties

Black N Brew is a neighborhood full scale coffee shop with homemade cafe style food. We prepare all of our food on premise. We believe in providing yummy meals that satisfy but don't stuff you to the gills.  We offer a creative menu with plenty of healthy options for all. There are plenty of vegetarian options as well as a few vegan treats offered on any given day. We have free wifi and are dog friendly. Black n Brew PROUDLY serves La Colombe coffee and Metropolitan Bread (comes in fresh daily). We really take pride in what we do and wanted to create a place where anyone could come and feel welcome and inspired! Also if you are into art we feature monthly local artist work on our walls and the outside of our building is a mosaic by Isaiah Zagar.

History

Established in 2007.

Black N Brew is a staple on East Passyunk Ave. Fellow business owners and neighborhood people love Black n Brew as their meeting spot! You can find, old south Philly, new homeowners, business owners, real estate agents and hipsters all enjoying coffee and a bite to eat on any given day.

Meet the Business Owner

Colleen Jen D. Business Owner

You can find Jen or myself at the cafe almost any day. We are working owners and we take pride in what we do. It is our goal to continue Black N Brew's mission of creating a place where anyone can feel welcomed and inspired. We spend so much time at the shop and we know how important it is to get a creamy latte and a yummy hot breakfast that when we go else where and that is not delivered we get upset. So we do our best to make sure that it is done right every time at Black N Brew.  Black n Brew has been a dream come true for me. Inspiring environment, good-simple eats, hot coffee and espresso drinks all done with a smile. We believe in customer service!
