        I went to Fuji Mountain for the first time in years mainly due to the fact that I had found a great deal on LivingSocial.com a few months back ($15 for a $30 gift certificate). This place definitely didn't disappoint!

        There are some comments on here saying the menu is a bit pricey, but I disagree considering the location and the freshness of the food. (Though I do think the drinks are overpriced.) For $36, my friend and I split two appetizers and three sushi rolls, which really didn't seem too expensive (especially with the $30 gift certificate). For the appetizers, we ordered the edamame (which I'm not a fan of in any restaurant) and the wassabi shumai, which was absolutely fantastic! The shumai had just the right amount of heat and was steamed to perfection - definitely one of my new favorites. As of the sushi rolls, we got the crazy (tuna, eel, cucumber, avocado, shrimp tempura and masago ), the J.B.S. (avocado and cream cheese roll topped with salmon), and the shrimp tempura rolls. The crazy roll was only four pieces, but definitely a delicious mouthful - we both really enjoyed it! The other two rolls were good and fresh, but definitely nothing to write home about.

        Overall, we had a really good experience here and would recommend it to others. The wait staff is very friendly and available, the food is pretty good, and the restaurant's atmosphere is inviting and quiet. Go check it out!
