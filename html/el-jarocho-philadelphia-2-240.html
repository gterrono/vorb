    One of those places that makes you want to move to the neighborhood. Not fancy in any way, but who cares if the food is tasty? I particularly like the tacos - I've tried the chorizo and chicken-stuffed pepper ones. First of all, seasoned chicken inside of a deep fried jalepeño is wonderful enough. But then these people had the audacity to put that into a taco. May Jah pour blessings in this restaurant's direction. I also enjoyed an enchilada with mole sauce once. I really like exploring their menu - a lot of great options!

    I also recommend ordering an horchata - their version is heavy on cinnamon, which was a pleasant surprise.

    I look forward to coming here many more times in the future.

    I feel very compelled to write something after tonight's awesome burrito.

    Yes, I really don't think they speak much English.  Trust me that's probably a good thing in terms of their food.

    Yes, their service is lacking.  Get over it, this isn't a white tablecloth and candlelight kind of place.  You get your food, eat it because its awesome, and get out.  

    This is a great Mexican restaurant.  The people that call in to order all speak Spanish to them.  I know because I speak Spanish and that's all I heard while I was waiting on my to go order.  If you think about that for a minute it might hit you as a good sign.  When was the last time you went into a Chinese to-go type of place and heard to go orders coming in by phone from people who speak Chinese?  I'd have to think pretty damn hard.... Maybe in SF Chinatown.  

    I had a carna asada burrito and was like a koala crapped a rainbow in my brain, then melted in my mouth.

    Go here.  Or not, I actually don't really care.  In fact I hope you don't so I won't have to wait too long.  

    I just know I found my Mexican place in Philadelphia that really hits the spot.

    Definitely my favorite authentic Mexican in south philly.

    I've tried tacos, burritos, and tortas. All done extremely well for a good price. The inside is small but ok. Not good for big groups. It's BYOB which is nice. I wish they had more tables. Sometimes two people will sit at a four top and you'll have nowhere to sit. I'd definitely recommend eating there at an off hour or just getting take out.

    Atmosphere inside is exactly what you'd expect at any Mexican joint. Basically a Mexican version of a pizza place.

    There is one server there who never looks happy, but you didn't do anything wrong. If she doesn't smile don't take it personally.

    I always get prompt delivery here. Would recommend to anyone!
