    Great place to grab a gluten-free (and, if you're so inclined, vegetarian) lunch and not feel like you're eating gluten-free. This place is literally "grab-and-go" so sandwiches/salads and plates are pre-made so you can, well, grab and go. Fear not though -  you definitely aren't compromising on freshness or taste.  

    The sandwiches are made with a couple of different kinds of gluten-free bread depending on the option. I have tried gluten-free bread before and had since given up on bread altogether - until now.  There is a coffee bar and soup as well.

    My veggie sandwich was excellent. Filling and the perfect combination of flavors. If you are a "big portion" type of person, you may be a little disappointed with the portion sizes but I have to tell you that as a person who actually portions out all of their food - the portions here are on point with what you *should* be eating in a mid-day meal. For those of us who care about such things - all food options have the calorie count on them and if you are interested in going that extra step, other nutritional values are listed on their website. I couldn't have asked for more.

    They also have baked goods with gluten-free, vegan, and even dairy-free options. I had a bit of the Sweet Potato brownie and I must say, if you're a brownie lover with dietary restrictions, you no longer have to give up on that sweet treat.  

    Give this place a try - even if you aren't a health nut, you'll love your meal!
