    Listed in Viva la Mexico!

    I am a bonafide California girl and most East Coast taquerias put me off, usually because of the prices.  Usually I wouldn't give an eatery that I primarily use for take-out as a 5-star, but I have been in search of an AUTHENTIC Mexican place that serves up great mexican dishes at reasonable prices. This place is my new favorite spot with authentic Oaxacan-style mexican food, where you can be completely full (and even have leftovers) for ten dollars or less.

    They have everything from tacos to tortas to burritos to enchiladas to sopes.  I haven't found sopes since I left California, those thick pueblo corn tortillas topped with shredded chicken or beef, sour cream, lettuce, and salsa.  The mole burrito is amazing and also try the pork & pineapple burrito.  For dessert try the carmelized plaintains in sweet condensed milk.

    The total cost was like less $15 for sure.
    They also deliver if it is just too darn cold to go for a pick up.
    I also noticed on the menu if you buy a certain amount of burritos you get one free. 6 i think?
    Will definitely try it again.
