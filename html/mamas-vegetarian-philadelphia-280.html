    I think I got the general $9 or $10 Mama's platter, which has a little bit of everything- falafel balls, latke, fried eggplant, warm pita, and a gigantic heap of cucumber-tomato salad covered in a delicious tzatziki-ish sauce.

    The falafel was delicious and crunchy and the platter offered plenty of veggies to load up and make a pita sandwich with.
