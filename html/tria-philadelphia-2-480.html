    My husband and I stopped by Tria for an impromptu dinner after seeing the great reviews on Yelp and we weren't disappointed. Although they don't take reservations, we arrived just after 6:30 on a Friday night and only had to wait five minutes for an outside table. Our service was impeccable a from beginning to end. Our main waitress was Becky. She was extremely knowledgeable about the menu items and at our request helped us make our selections. We were also attended to by three other servers or staff members, all of whom seemed genuinely interested in making our experience enjoyable.  All of them seemed to be enjoying their work.

    We started off with wine and a couple of excellent cheeses (there was an extensive list of both from which to choose). One of the cheeses was paired with a red beet spread, which was fabulous, and the other with honey, and both came with sliced baguettes. Next we had the Smoked Duck and Spinach Salad with Pistachios, Allagash cherries, shaved Grana Padano cheese and Citrus-Mint Vinaigrette. It was delicious. We also had the warm Mission Figs with Gorganzola and Prosciutto. We both loved the prosciutto but thought the figs would have been better with more cheese as the sweetness overpowered the very faint cheese flavor. The amazing finale to our delightful outdoor dining experience (70 degrees at 7pm in mid-October--although there were patio heaters if we would've wanted them) was the warm Brioche Bread Pudding with Banana and Caramel, topped with cold, creamy Honey Marscapone. We left full and excited to have found a wonderful tapas and wine bar in the heart of downtown Philly.

Specialties

Welcome to Tria, Philadelphia's wine, cheese and beer cafe. We serve real wines, cheeses and beers crafted by passionate people. We relentlessly seek out the best products both locally and from around the world. We dig flavor. Our ever-changing fermentation menus are complemented by a cafe menu served from opening to late night. Don't know the difference between Cabernet and Carmignano? Don't worry. Our friendly, knowledgeable staff will help guide you through a memorable wine, cheese and beer experience.
