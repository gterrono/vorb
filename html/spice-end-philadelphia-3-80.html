    I've been waiting to find a good Kathi Roll place in Philadelphia, and finally I moved to Center City and happened across Spice End the other day. Unfortunately, it looks like my search has to continue.

    Don't get me wrong, Spice End is good. They have a decent selection of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian rolls, but the prices are a tad high and the rolls themselves aren't anything too special.

    I tried the paneer tikka roll (cottage cheese with spices), the paneer and aloo (cottage cheese with potatoes), the egg roll, and the mixed vegetable roll. Of the 4, my favorite were by and far the paneer tikka and mixed veggie. They were well spiced and good eats, but the heaping amount of dhania (cilantro) chutney put over top was very off-putting. On my second visit I asked them to lessen the chutney amount, but it was still too much, and I wonder if they even made any changes.

    In all, not bad, but the chutney ratio and generally high prices for the food make this an average place in my opinion. Kudos to them for having Limca - a nice refreshing lemon drink by Coca Cola more common to East Asia.

History

Established in 2013.

Intorducing Spice End - Kati Roll and Platters
Spice End Kati Roll and platters located in Central Philly resonates the enthusiasm, flavor, authenticity and vivaciousness of the city. It represents Indian cuisine with a twist. Amidst the hustle of the city this place gives you an option of having food full of flavors at affordable prices which you can eat while walking, sitting or over a conversation with a friend, in hurry or at peace in the restaurant. Spice End redefines Indian Street food on Streets of Central Philly engulfing the modernization of its surroundings.

Kati Rolls: The essence of this place.
For those who are new to this concept, KATI Rolls are soft, warm perfectly rolled Indian flatbread, stuffed with variety of fillings such as vegetables, chicken, lamb, beef, potatoes and cottage cheese, which are perfectly marinated in mouthwatering Indian spices. Our rolls are served with special sauces which add the oomph element and complete the food.
