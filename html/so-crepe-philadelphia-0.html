    So crepe...so amazing. (Sorry, couldn't resist)

    I love crepes and when this place opened up I really wanted to get in there. It's a cute place, beautifully done with an open kitchen, its muy clean. They have a savory crepe menu and a sweet crepe menu, awesome. What I love is that they didn't go over board with tons of option, rather a handful in each menu.  Even then I still had a hard time deciding and got one of each. The prices, very reasonable: $4-$9 per.

    I opted for the so classic - egg and cheese on a crepe. The best part was the ketchup, its a whipped ketchup-cream that was just amazing. I had to get the so chocolate as well, and it was good too. I'm usually all about the chocolate crepes, but here the egg and cheese with the fancy ketchup really trumps it. And just great service in general.

    I was really tempted to get a third, but I'll have to wait another day. Viva la crepe!

    My husband and I live in the Graduate Hospital neighborhood and have been looking forward to the opening of So Crepe for a few months. We went opening day for dinner and had a wonderful time! The two owners are charming and friendly; they were very welcoming as they came to great us at our table and personally open our wine (So Crepe is a BYOB).

    The savory crepes are delicious and are not your run of the mill crepes. My husband got the So Riviera (shrimp, parmesan and spinach) and I got the So Cocotte (chicken with a rich mustard sauce). The only downsize was that we expected smaller portions and almost didn't have room for our delicious sweet crepes for desert!

    Definitely a wonderful addition to the growing neighborhood!
