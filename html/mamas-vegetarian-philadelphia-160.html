    Four stars because that, in my opinion is the most any place should qualify for if it isn't fine dinning at its best.  Mama's is the best falafel in Philadelphia. Period.     Listed in Philly - As good as it gets

    Do you like falafel? Do you like sabich (pronounced sa-beekh - the kh indicates a gutteral ugh sound)?

    Well, if you do, then you need to eat at Mama's. Best of both in Philly.

    Authentic (I'd know)... hummus that's smooth the way it should be (who-moose, or if you're feeling especially ambitious, kho-moose), tahina that adds the flavor you're expecting (it's not tahini, despite common belief, for the ambitious, try ta-khina), and israeli salad that adds the juiciness and crunch you need (read diced tomatoes and cucumbers, they leave out the peppers here).

    The sabich includes some eggplant and a hard-boiled egg... it makes my heart sing. They've even got whole wheat pitas for those being extra health-conscious (it's already Kosher, so you've got one star in the health department going in).

    If you don't like falafel or sabich, or haven't tried them, come to Mama's and you'll love it like I do. Try and make a liar out of me.
