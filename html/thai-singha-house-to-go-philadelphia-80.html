    One glorious weekday afternoon that I had off I decided to check this place out, as I am a Thai food enthusiast and I've heard about this place delivering on quality, cheap, good eats. A short walk from my house, I found this teenie tiny little Thai "hut" with friendly people behind the counter and the scents of some delicious Thai food filling the room. How have I not been here before?

    I opted for an item that wasn't on the $6 lunch special list, but I only paid $7.50 for it, so I wasn't feeling particularly bad about the extra $1.50. Especially after I received my meal and it was a HUGE steaming hot portion of noodles, veggies, and chicken. I got the Pad Sui Ew, one of my Thai go-tos, it was perfectly prepared. I also have an entire second meal in the fridge for lunch today.

    This seems to be the perfect lunch spot. There are like 3 stools in the window, but this is really a true carry out place (hence the "to-go" in the restaurant name). If you go just past peak lunch hours you will not wait at all, I walked in without any line at all. Plus I called ahead. Total win.

    I will very very much be looking forward to going back and trying the soups, the currys, and the other noodle dishes.

    I was on my way home from work and needed to pick up takeout so I searched Yelp for "takeout" in the area and this was one of the results!  I had never been here before but figured I should give it a try.  I'm so glad I did.

    I ordered the fried tofu appetizer, the drunken noodles with chicken, wondered what curry to get and settled on the red curry with duck.  Everything was ready very fast.  My only hesitation from having read the reviews was that people mentioned a lot of things are pre-made, but they were definitely cooking things up then and there, and cooked the duck at that time.  They even thought to package the duck separately from the red curry so that the skin would stay crispy and the whole thing wouldn't get soggy.  The woman behind the counter was really friendly and chatted with me, as well as with regulars who were coming in to pick up orders they had phoned in.  

    The red curry was a bit on the bland/sweet side for me (but with lovely coconut milk flavor), but the duck was great.  The drunken noodles were very tasty -- nice and spicy -- and had a good texture.  

    I would definitely order from here again!
