    Hit up Circles with my dad after a Phils game (thanks Yelp Mobile App) and had a nice dinner.  We were the only people in the place at 9:30 on a Monday night, so service was mellow and good.
    Tasty fresh rolls - got the vegetarian version as my dad does not eat meat.  Also ordered the Red curry chicken.  When I asked for "hot" the server asked me, "You want 'hot' or 'Thai hot'?" Well, Thai hot, obviously.  Boy was that some spicy curry.  The kind of hot that starts mellow and finishes like a beast, but leaves you with the desire for more.  My dad had the green curry tofu, also "Thai hot," and we spent the meal eating and sniffing away as it wasn't the kind of heat that made us sweat but definitely cleared out our sinuses!
    Dad also ordered a Thai burrito to go for Mom, which he said they enjoyed the next day.

    Good meal, the right level of spice for us, and didn't break the bank at all.  Next time I'm nearby , I'll be back for more.
