        We were looking for a BYOB for dinner, this place came up on Yelp. It is NOT a BYO. Our waitress told us that she could not open our bottle for us or give us wine glasses because they have a full bar. However, the food was very good.

        We started with complementary vegetable dip and bread. The dip was zucchini, onions, and other vegetables covered in olive oil. It was light and delicious. We ordered the mussels with red sauce as an appetizer, and out came a HUGE bowl. I've never seen such a big portion served at a restaurant. For the entree, I had the papardelle porcini. I never had that kind of flat, thick pasta before, but it was divine. The sauce was savory and really complemented the mushrooms. I would have licked my plate clean if I thought no one would see. They have outdoor seating and our servers were attentive and friendly. After living in Rome for a semester, I'm pretty picky about Italian food. Spasso definitely passed the test.

        I can't believe this place has been around forever and I never visited until last night. We were able to get the window seat by the front door, the booth was comfy enough to accomodate 5 people. The interior was filled with dark mahogany and hues of red, the space was massive, there is a huge room in the back to accomodate private parties as well as a room housing a long wooden bar.  Although, the space was large, it was quite cozy!
        My co-workers took me out for dinner and all decided that this was the place that was going to have something for everyone in our group! I was not disappointed. Our waitress was very nice, however I think she was new or a trainee, she seemed very nervous and a little bit uneasy.  My collegues mentioned that usually the wait staff is very bubbly and a highlight of the meal, but she was fine and service was wonderful.

        We started off with the goat cheese and walnut salad, the honey balsamic dressing was delicious, it was a really simple salad, but I enjoyed it emmensely!  The caesar salad also go rave reviews from the group.  The grilled calamari was so tasty, I would have eaten another order of that, you could really taste the smoky flavor from the grill and the texture was perfect.
        I split the branzino with another and the portion was perfect, it was a nice clean fluffy white fish with a side of grilled vegetables, I can't say it was really flavorful, but it was cooked well, but nothing to write home about.  The real stars of the night were the  homemade pastas. Papardelle Porcini that my co-worker ordered the pasta was homemade with a light brown butter cream sauce with chicken and sun dried tomatoes had me wishing that I had ordered that!  shrimp diablo was well recieved as well, jumbo shrimp mixed in a spicy red sauce was a perfect combination, not to mention filling! I came home wanting to make my next reservation so I can have my own entree of the Papardelle!  Even through we came right after work, I hear that this place is quite the hopping dining destination on the weekends!
        I will definitely be stopping in here again, the prices are reasonable and the atmosphere was nice and relaxed.
