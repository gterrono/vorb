    Mmm Sampan, how can I put into words how much I love you? I used to live across the street and was definitely a frequent flier at this fantastic place. Not one bad meal to date, although it's kind of crazy during sips Wednesdays in the summer so maybe that's not the best night to check it out.

    The chefs tasting is definitely the way to go, and only gets better with more people in your party! So many options for dishes and you get to try so much!
