    We ate there on a Friday night for the first time and we were so impressed we brought friends back there the next night! The Vaca Frita was outstanding, skirt steak, marinated, tender, crispy on the outside with rice, delicious black beans and their red sauce with fresh slices of avocado. The next night I had the scallops, very tasty.
    The tuna tacos appetizer are a must. Smoky, mini tacos with shredded cabbage. Get an order just for yourself. (5 to an order)
    I have to mention the lovely hostess Alexandria for encouraging us to come in even though Saturday night proved to have no reservations until 10 pm. There are 3 floors of seating, we sat on the second floor on Friday night but Alexandria was able to get us seated in their lounge on the first floor which when I go back I would prefer as the atmosphere is so cozy in the whole restaurant but very elegant with great ambiance in the lounge. The lighting and comfortable, stuffed chairs make it easy to eat your meal at the table.
    Janelle was our server, WOW is the overall service spectacular there and Janelle was a part of the superb attention the staff pays to each customer. She really knows her rum suggestions also. She's friendly, very helpful, not over bearing ( nothing worse than being constantly interrupted while dining with friends) and a trait I feel is lost in restaurants these days, the ability to serve a table and be "invisible". It was such a treat to have water glasses always full, drinks replenished, plates cleared, table clean, like magic. So, to re-cap: eat in the lounge, get the tuna tacos appetizer and Vaca Frita, Oh! And try their delicious Mojitos and Mango Martini!

    We then proceeded to order a medley of ceviche. You can order them solo, or in multiples of 3, 5, 7 to share. We tried the thai mixto, ecuadorian shrimp and long island fluke. They were all refreshing, amazing.

Stephen S. Business Owner

Founded in 1995 by Stephen Starr, Philadelphia-based STARR Restaurants is one of the fastest growing multi-concept restaurant companies in the country. STARR Restaurants include Buddakan, Morimoto, The Continental, Barclay Prime, Continental Mid-Town, El Vez, Jones, Alma de Cuba, Pod, Parc, Butcher and Singer, Pizzeria Stella, El Rey, Ranstead Room, The Dandelion, Talula's Garden, Frankford Hall, Il Pittore, Route 6 and seasonal food-stand SquareBurger in Philadelphia, as well as, Morimoto and Buddakan in New York City's thriving Meatpacking District. STARR Restaurants expanded to Atlantic City, NJ, with Buddakan and The Continental located in the Pier Shops at Caesars in 2006, and to Florida with Steak 954 at the W Hotel Fort Lauderdale in 2009, and Makoto in the Bal Harbour Shops in 2011.
