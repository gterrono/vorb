    Welcome to the neighborhood Kabuki- we're so happy you're here! Chinatown has been in need of a great sushi place, and I don't mean a Chinese/Japanese/Thai combo place. It's small inside, probably about 6 tables + sushi bar, with a very clean/modern decor. To drink they offer a variety of Boba Tea's, fruit juices and smoothies. I ordered the mango green tea and it was absolutely delicious. They have a great variety of specialty rolls and regular favorites. I ordered the Pink Lady and Fantasy rolls, both were fantastic. The Pink Lady is huge and comes with 10 pieces. The fish was extremely fresh and the overall roll was beautifully colored. The Fantasy roll was also just as tasty. I would definitely order them both again. They do a beautiful job plating the dishes with vegetable decorations and cool lighting. Get here soon during their grand opening and receive 20%. And remember to BYOB!

    I was walking around center city with my girlfriend looking for a place to eat and saw Kabuki. I was amazed on how it looked from the inside because on the outside, it is surrounded by a dull environment.

    The servers noticed us quickly and welcomed us with a big smile. They gave us time and also recommendations on what to get as a starter.

    We ordered the Spicy Crabmeat Tartar to start and also two drinks -- Jasmine Milk Tea and Mango Tea without the tapioca. The Spicy Crabmeat Tartar tasted amazing! I have no idea what I ate, but you have to try it to believe it. You cannot go wrong to start with this appetizer. Trust me! The Jasmine Milk Tea also was amazing!! There was a jasmine taste to it and not too sweet. The Mango tea was decent. Not much to say about it.

    Next, we ordered three special rolls -- Pink Lady, Best of the Best, and Crazy Friday roll. All the sushi came in on one plate with fancy lights and decorated nicely. I called it the, "Noah's Ark" because it was decorated as a boat and  sushi surrounding it. It was too pretty to eat as my girlfriend said. However, we devoured it and there was no complaints whatsoever. It was served perfectly and also presented amazingly. Couldn't be any happier with the food here and also the service.

    Price is fairly high for a sushi but it was definitely worth every cent! Good service and good food. Even my stomach feels good afterwards! Definitely will be back for more.

    Highly recommended if you are craving sushi! My tip is to start with the Spicy Crabmeat Tartar!!

Specialties

Located just down the street from the Convention Center, Kabukiza Sushi has become the premier destination for sushi and Japanese food in the Chinatown area of Philadelphia. We serve only the highest-quality fish, and the most original sushi rolls in the area. Our inviting atmosphere and friendly staff have quickly made Kabukiza Sushi a neighborhood favorite.

History

Established in 2013.

A brand new addition to Center City, Kabukiza Sushi is proud to bring the best sushi and Japanese food available to the area. We are quickly becoming known as a unique and exciting alternative to other sushi restaurants. Whether you are looking for an authentic restaurant to share a meal with friends or a great date option, Kabukiza Sushi provides the perfect backdrop for any occasion. Our goal is to serve fresh, delicious food in a warm and inviting atmosphere.
