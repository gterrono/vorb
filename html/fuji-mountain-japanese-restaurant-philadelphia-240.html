    There's a very zen-like atmosphere to the restaurant which can be nice after a long week of work or a stressful day. I ordered the Seafood Miso Soup and the Inferno Roll (spicy tuna, crunch, salmon, spicy yellowtail, masago, & spicy sauce). The soup was actually pretty good albeit was a little salty which was a little disappointing because in my mind seafood and miso should balance out relatively well. As for the Inferno Roll, I don't think all the "spicy" in the description is anything you should be intimidated by (although I have a rather high tolerance for hot foods); the roll was really good although a little hard to eat with all the stuff stacked on top of the roll - good nonetheless. The only thing that turned me off was price - the Inferno Roll was $15 for 8 pieces & there are many much more expensive "special rolls" than that one - but I'm new to city-living and I know things can be costly. Oh, I hear they have karaoke upstairs too, not that I tried it out.

    First, Fuji Mountain participates in Sips, so going there on a Wednesday night was awesome.
    Second, THEIR SUSHI IS PHENOMENAL! My boyfriend and I shared 3 rolls: spicy tuna (his choice) and the Florida and Fire roll (my choices). Specialties

Authentic Japanese Cuisine specializing in high quality & fresh sushi. We are an award winning sushi oasis offering lunch, dinner, late-night & private karaoke parties

Our fourth floor karaoke room is ideal for private parties, and can accommodate up to 30 people. It offers the same full menu as our other floors, and is a great place to let loose and enjoy the night. Direct access to the bar is only a few steps away, and European bottle service is available. The room can be rented anytime from 6PM - 2AM daily, and reservations are strongly recommended.

History

Established in 1995.

Fuji Mountain prides itself with serving only the freshest & highest quality fish in all of Philadelphia. Where great service meets excellent fish.

Meet the Manager

Anthony S. Manager
