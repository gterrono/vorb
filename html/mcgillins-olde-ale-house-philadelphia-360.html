        Listed in The Great Philly Food Experience

        Is there anything left to say about McGillin's Old Ale House that hasn't already been said or written?  ... prob not.  So I'll make this review short.

         McGillins is one of America's oldest pubs and Philadelphia's oldest continuously operating pub.  It's also one of the world's most crowded.  It's located on a cool hidden back alley called Drury.  At one time or another every young dude in Philly has been drunk in McGillins.  At one point or another every young girl in Philly has been drunk in McGillins.  Yes, at night the crowd might seem too young for some folks ... but hang in there (or go during the day!).  It's worth it even if you are of an age where you no longer need to produce an ID to drink.  There's good food ...great beers ... all the history beneath its timbered ceiling ... and remember - there's truly only one McGillin's Old Ale House in this entire world!

        McGillin's is indisputably my favorite watering hole in America. Besides being the best purveyor of $6.50 regulation sized pitchers and $.35 delicious jumbo wings in the city, it's environment is unmatched. The history, sense of community and general good spirits that spew from the neon lighted placard on Drury St. can alter your mood on the gloomiest of days. Would you like a $37 specialty martini for a quarter the price and twice the deliciousness? McGillins has you covered. Had a long day at the office and just want to unwind with friends? McGillins has you covered. Want to watch a game on a weekday and money is tight? Wing Night on tuesdays has you covered. Speaking of cover, McGillins has none. I used to go to McGillin's every tuesday for wing night while attending college in the city and every time I stepped  through the wooden doors I was greeted by the amazing staff as if I were royalty. After being removed from Philly for three years due to work, I recently walked back into McGillins and not only did the staff recognize me, but I was greeted with hugs and general interest about what I had been up to. McGillin's is an incredibly special place whether its completely empty or packed to the brim. There is something special about this place that brings people closer together.  McGillin's is amazing, the food is cheap, the beer is cheap and you will have a good time guaranteed.

      Specialties

      Philadelphia's Oldest Pub, featuring 29 beers on draft focusing on local, fresh micro brews.  A pub menu loaded with homemade items, everything from our famous Nachos and French Onion Soup to the Irish Mixed Grill and Cape May Stew.

      McGillin's is considered the BEST place for karaoke in the city - Sundays and Wednesdays are hopping with DJ Joe at the mic.

      Meet the Business Owner

      Chris M. Business Owner

      Christopher Mullins, Jr. runs McGillin's Olde Ale House with his parents, Mary Ellen & Chris Mullins, Sr. They purchased it from Mary Ellen's father & uncle (Christopher's grandfather & great-uncle). They're only the second family to run the historic tavern in its 150+ year history. McGillin's is the oldest continuously operating tavern in Philadelphia and one of the oldest in the country. Christopher believes that beer is the eternal equalizer - friends have been made, marriages begun, wars ended. If we can ignite camaraderie and friendship than we have done our job!
