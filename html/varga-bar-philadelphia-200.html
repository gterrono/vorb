    I'm really glad some friends suggested Varga bar for dinner the other night--we were able to sample their ample tap list and have a yummy appetizer feast.

    We got the confit wings (tangy with slight heat, very satisfying), mussels (tender, tasty broth full of goodies), mac and cheese (show stealer), brussel sprouts and bacon (YUM! especially with the mac n cheese), sliders, and lamb lettuce wraps.

I felt like it was a 3.5 star experience, but because I had such a good time I had no doubt about rounding up. I'll be back.

    After a half-dozen delectable burgers, I figured I should try some other options.  Everything was good, and most things were amazing!  I tried chicken wings cooked in pomegranate juice and chili oil that were so tender the meat literally fell off the bone (I didn't even know you could do that with chicken wings).  Their macaroni and cheese bathes--no, luxuriates--in a three cheese sauce featuring gruyere, marscapone and fontina swirled with truffle oil and topped with bacon.  Sunday brunch was also fantastic (though the burger steals the show on that menu too).

    I had an only-average experience here once, and I'm willing to write that off as a fluke after over a dozen great meals.
